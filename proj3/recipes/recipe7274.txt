Recipe By:Linda Waddy "This cake is a Hawaiian favorite. Make your favorite chocolate cake. The real secret is in the frosting." Ingredients 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon evaporated milk 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon white sugar 1-1/4 egg yolks, beaten 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon butter 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon flaked coconut 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon chopped macadamia nuts Directions Combine milk, sugar, egg yolks, butter and vanilla in a saucepan. Cook on medium heat until thick, approximately 12 minutes. Stir constantly. Remove from heat, and add coconut and macadamia nuts. Continue to stir until cool and thick enough to spread on your cake. Frost. This will cover a 9x13 inch rectangular cake or a two 8 inch layer round cake.
