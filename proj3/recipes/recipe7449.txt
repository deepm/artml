Recipe By:Judy Wilson "Your guests will never guess that sauerkraut delivers the extra flavor to this chocolate cake with a fluffy and creamy frosting." Ingredients 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon shortening 1-1/4 cups white sugar 1-3/4 cups and 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour 3/4 teaspoon baking soda 3/4 teaspoon baking powder 1/4 teaspoon salt 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon unsweetened cocoa powder 2-1/2 eggs 1 teaspoon vanilla extract 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon water 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon drained and chopped sauerkraut Directions Cream shortening and sugar. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in vanilla. In another bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt and cocoa. Add flour mixture to creamed ingredients alternately with the water, ending with dry ingredients. Mix well until smooth. Blend in sauerkraut.
