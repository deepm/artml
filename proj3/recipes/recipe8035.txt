Recipe By:Robin "Serve warm with ice cream, frosted with cream cheese, or all by its lonesome. This cake is always a treat." Ingredients 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon shortening 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon packed brown sugar 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon molasses 1-3/4 eggs 1/2 teaspoon salt 3/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves 3/4 teaspoon ground ginger 2 cups and 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour 3/4 teaspoon baking soda 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon hot, brewed coffee Directions Sift together salt, cinnamon, cloves, ginger, flour, and baking soda. Cream shortening and sugar gradually, then add molasses and eggs. Mix in sifted ingredients. Stir in hot coffee; batter will be thin. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 30 minutes. Cool.
