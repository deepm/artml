Recipe By:Jenn Hall "Dipping the butter-brushed tops of these muffins in a mixture of cinnamon and sugar is easy way to spruce-up a basic muffin recipe." Ingredients 1-2/3 cups all-purpose flour 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon white sugar 2-1/2 teaspoons baking powder 1/2 teaspoon salt 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons milk 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon vegetable oil 7/8 egg 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons melted butter 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon white sugar 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon Directions Heat oven to 400 degrees F (205 degrees C). Grease bottoms only of 12 muffin cups or line with baking cups. In a medium bowl, combine flour, 1/2 cup sugar, baking powder and salt; mix well. In a small bowl, combine milk, oil and egg; blend well. Add dry ingredients all at once; stir just until dry ingredients are moistened (batter will be lumpy.) Bake for 20 to 25 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. Cool 1 minute before removing from pan. Brush tops of hot muffins with 2 tablespoons melted butter.
