Recipe By:knowell "Absolutely the best! Soft crust and well risen." Ingredients 1 cup and 1 tablespoon sourdough starter 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon water 2-1/2 cups all-purpose flour 2-1/2 teaspoons white sugar 2-1/2 teaspoons vegetable oil 3/4 teaspoon salt 1-3/4 teaspoons bread machine yeast Directions Allow starter to come to room temperature. Place all ingredients in the bread machine in the order suggested by the manufacturer. Select Basic or White Bread cycle, and Medium crust setting. Start.
