Recipe By:Carol "This is a superb icing and does not take long to make." Ingredients 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon butter 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons lemon juice 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract 2-1/2 cups confectioners' sugar Directions Combine the butter, lemon juice, vanilla, and confectioner's sugar and beat until light and fluffy.
