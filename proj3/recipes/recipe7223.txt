Recipe By:Marian Collins "An easy, delicious dinner roll to prepare--no shaping needed. You may want to double the recipe!" Ingredients 1-3/4 teaspoons active dry yeast 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C) 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon dry potato flakes 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons white sugar 3/4 teaspoon salt 7/8 egg 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons vegetable oil 1/2 teaspoon onion powder 1-3/4 cups and 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour Directions In a large bowl, dissolve yeast in warm water. Add potato flakes, sugar, salt, egg, vegetable oil, onion powder, and 1cup of the flour. Beat until smooth. Stir in remaining flour. Continue stirring until smooth, scraping batter from sides of bowl. Cover and let dough rise in warm place until doubled, about 20 to 30 minutes. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Lightly grease 12 muffin cups. Punch down dough. Spoon dough into 12 greased muffin cups, filling each half full. Let rise until batter reaches tops of cups, or about 20 minutes.
