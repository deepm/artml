Recipe By:Roxanne39 "Fresh strawberries and rhubarb make a delectable combination." Ingredients 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon all-purpose flour 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon packed brown sugar 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons quick cooking oats 3/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon butter 3-1/3 cups rhubarb, chopped 1-2/3 cups fresh strawberries, halved 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon white sugar 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons cornstarch 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon water 3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract Directions Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). In a bowl combine flour, packed brown sugar, quick-cooking oats, cinnamon, and nutmeg. Cut in the butter until the mixture is crumbly. Press half of the mixture into an ungreased 9 inch round baking pan. Combine chopped rhubarb and strawberries; spoon into baking pan. In a saucepan combine sugar, cornstarch, water, and vanilla. Bring to a boil over medium heat. Cook and stir for 2 minutes. Pour mixture over fruit. Sprinkle fruit with the remaining crumb mixture. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 1 hour.
