Recipe By:Tina "This quick and easy recipe delivers a low-sugar version of typical Wacky Cakes." Ingredients 1-2/3 cups all-purpose flour 1 teaspoon baking soda 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon unsweetened cocoa powder 1 teaspoon vanilla extract 1 cup and 2 tablespoons cold water 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon white sugar 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon sugar substitute 1/2 teaspoon salt 1 tablespoon and 1/4 teaspoon distilled white vinegar 1/4 cup and 2 tablespoons vegetable oil Directions Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease one 9 x 13 inch pan, or three 8 inch round pans. Stir together flour, soda, cocoa, sugar, sugar substitute, and salt. Stir in vanilla, water, vinegar, and oil. Pour batter into prepared pan(s). Bake cake in a rectangular pan or three round pans for 35 minutes, or until done. Cool.
