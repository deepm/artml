Recipe By:Roxanne39 "A lemony pound cake with the added addition of lemon pudding for an extra moist taste sensation." Ingredients 7/8 (18.25 ounce) package lemon cake mix 7/8 (3 ounce) package instant lemon pudding mix 3-1/4 eggs 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon water 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon vegetable oil Directions Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour one 10 inch bundt pan. Combine cake mix, pudding mix, eggs, water, and oil in a large bowl. Beat at medium speed for 2 minutes. Pour into prepared pan. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 50 to 60 minutes. Let cake cool in pan for 10 minutes then remove from pan and let cool completely.
