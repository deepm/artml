Recipe By:Debbie "By definition, dump cakes are simple and fuss-free to make. Try this recipe using fresh blueberries and cake mix for a dessert everyone will love." Ingredients 1/2 (18.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix 2-1/4 cups fresh blueberries 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon white sugar 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon butter, melted 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon Directions Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Mix berries, sugar, and cinnamon in the bottom of a 9 x 13 inch pan. Cover berries with dry cake mix. Pour butter over cake mix, do not stir. Bake for 30 minutes, or until light brown.
