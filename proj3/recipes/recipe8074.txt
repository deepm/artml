Recipe By:PJ Coward "This is a FLAVORFUL simple sugar glaze. Good for rich pound cakes." Ingredients 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons white sugar 1/4 cup and 1 tablespoon water 1/2 teaspoon coconut extract 1/2 teaspoon rum flavored extract 1/2 teaspoon butter flavored extract 1/2 teaspoon lemon extract 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract 1/2 teaspoon almond extract Directions Combine sugar, water, extracts, and flavorings in a saucepan. Bring to a boil, stirring until sugar is dissolved. Spoon hot glaze over cake. ALL RIGHTS RESERVED Â© 2019 Allrecipes.com Printed From Allrecipes.com 3/30/2019
