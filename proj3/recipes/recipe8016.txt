Recipe By:Geri "Prepared poppyseed filling and chopped nuts are mixed into a simple batter in this quick recipe." Ingredients 2 cups and 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour 1-1/3 cups and 2 tablespoons white sugar 1 cup and 1 tablespoon vegetable oil 2-3/4 eggs 3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract 1/4 teaspoon salt 1 teaspoon baking soda 3/4 (12 fluid ounce) can evaporated milk 6 ounces poppyseed filling 1 cup and 1 tablespoon chopped walnuts Directions Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour one 10 inch tube pan. Combine the flour, white sugar, vegetable oil, eggs, vanilla, salt, baking soda and evaporated milk. Mix until smooth. Add the jar of poppy seed filling and the chopped nuts; beat at medium speed for 2 minutes. Pour the batter into the prepared pan. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 70 minutes.
