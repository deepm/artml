Recipe By:Judy Wilson "This simple recipe makes a fluffy chocolate frosting you'll be happy to use on your best cakes, cupcakes, and even cookies." Ingredients 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon shortening 3-1/3 cups confectioners' sugar 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon dry milk powder 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon water 2-1/2 (1 ounce) squares unsweetened chocolate, melted 1/4 teaspoon salt 1-1/4 teaspoons vanilla extract 7/8 egg white Directions Cream together shortening and half of the confectioners' sugar. Mix together powdered milk and water. Add to the creamed mixture along with the rest of the confectioners' sugar. Mix well. Stir in the unsweetened chocolate, salt, vanilla and unbeaten egg white.
