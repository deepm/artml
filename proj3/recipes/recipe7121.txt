Recipe By:Kathy Kauffman "A nice texture and flavor, great with any Italian food. Grill with a little butter and garlic for garlic bread." Ingredients 2/3 cup warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C) 1 3/4 cups bread flour 2 teaspoons dry milk powder 2 teaspoons white sugar 1 teaspoon salt 1 tablespoon grated Parmesan cheese 1 1/2 teaspoons Italian seasoning 1 1/2 tablespoons margarine 1 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast Directions Place ingredients in the pan of the bread machine in the order recommended by the manufacturer. Select the Medium Crust and Basic/White Bread settings, and press Start.
