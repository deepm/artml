Recipe By:LITSTER5 "Bread machine recipe." Ingredients 1 cup and 1 tablespoon water 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons margarine 3/4 teaspoon salt 2-1/2 cups all-purpose flour 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon rolled oats 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons brown sugar 1-1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast Directions Add ingredients to bread machine pan in order recommended by your manufacturer.
