Recipe By:AUNTPT "Yet another way to utilize your sourdough starter. Try topping these yummy pancakes with fresh fruit and whipped cream. I hope you like them! The amount of water can be adjusted depending on the consistency of your starter." Ingredients 1-3/4 cups and 2 tablespoons sourdough starter 2-1/2 egg, beaten 1/4 cup and 1 tablespoon water 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons vegetable oil 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon nonfat dry milk powder 1-3/4 teaspoons salt 2-1/2 teaspoons baking soda 3 tablespoons and 2-1/4 teaspoons white sugar Directions In a large bowl, combine the sourdough starter, egg, water, and oil. In a separate bowl, combine the nonfat dry milk, salt, baking soda, and sugar. Stir to blend dry ingredients. Add to sourdough starter and mix until batter is smooth. Bake on a greased 350 degree F(175 degree C) griddle until golden brown on the bottom. Flip and bake on opposite side. ALL RIGHTS RESERVED © 2019 Allrecipes.com Printed From Allrecipes.com 3/29/2019
