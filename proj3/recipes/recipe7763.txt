Recipe By:Roxanne39 "Chocolate and coffee, one of the greatest flavor combinations of all time come together in this delicious cake." Ingredients 1/4 cup and 1 tablespoon shortening 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons white sugar 7/8 eggs 3/4 cup and 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour 1/4 teaspoon salt 1/8 teaspoon baking powder 1/2 teaspoon baking soda 1/2 cup and 1 teaspoon strong, hot, brewed coffee 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon unsweetened cocoa powder Directions Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour two 9 inch round cake pans. Cream the sugar and the shortening together until light. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each one. Sift the flour, salt, baking soda, and baking powder together. Set aside. Mix coffee and cocoa together until smooth. Add alternately with the flour mixture to the creamed mixture. Beat for 2 minutes then pour batter into the prepared pans. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 30 to 35 minutes. Frost when cooled.
