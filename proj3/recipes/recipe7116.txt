Recipe By:Dee "Kids and kids-at-heart love this! Make sure to use jelly, the sugar content in jam will hinder the success of this recipe. You may use any flavor jelly that you please." Ingredients 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon water 1 tablespoon and 3/4 teaspoon vegetable oil 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon peanut butter 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon blackberry jelly 2-1/2 teaspoons white sugar 3/4 teaspoon salt 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon whole wheat flour 1-2/3 cups bread flour 1-1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast Directions Place ingredients in the pan of the bread machine in the order suggested by the manufacturer. Select a Sweet, Raisin, or Basic setting. Start. ALL RIGHTS RESERVED © 2019 Allrecipes.com Printed From Allrecipes.com 3/29/2019
