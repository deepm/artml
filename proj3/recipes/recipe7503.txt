Recipe By:sassysquirrel "Chocolate cake with cola soda and a rich pecan frosting." Ingredients 1 cup and 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour 1 eggs 1 cup and 2 tablespoons white sugar 1/2 teaspoon baking soda 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon butter 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon miniature marshmallows 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons unsweetened cocoa powder 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon cola-flavored carbonated beverage 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon buttermilk 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon butter 1 tablespoon and 1/4 teaspoon unsweetened cocoa powder 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon cola-flavored carbonated beverage 2-1/4 cups confectioners' sugar 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon chopped pecans Directions Combine flour and white sugar in mixing bowl. Heat 1 cup butter, 3 tablespoons cocoa, and 1 cup cola soft drink to boiling and pour over the flour and sugar. Mix thoroughly. Add buttermilk, eggs, baking soda, vanilla, and marshmallows and mix well. Bake in a prepared 9 by 13 inch pan for 40 minutes at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). While still hot, frost with Coco Cola Frosting: Combine 1/2 cup butter or margarine, 2 tablespoons cocoa and 6 tablespoons cola soft drink and heat to boiling.
