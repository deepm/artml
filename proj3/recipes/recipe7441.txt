Recipe By:Michelle Dolorfino "A creamy chocolate frosting good for chocolate cakes. Makes enough to frost one layer cake." Ingredients 6 eggs 1 cup white sugar 3 (1 ounce) squares unsweetened chocolate, melted 3/4 cup butter Directions Combine eggs and sugar, and mix well. Cook in a double boiler, stirring constantly until thickened. Pour mixture into a medium size bowl, and add chocolate. Beat until cool. Beat in butter or margarine. Chill frosting until thick enough to spread.
