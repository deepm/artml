Recipe By:Doreen Wetheral "These are moist muffins with a hint of orange flavoring in it. I received this recipe from my great aunt at the time I was married." Ingredients 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon rolled oats 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon orange juice 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon boiling water 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon butter 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon packed brown sugar 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon white sugar 1-3/4 eggs, lightly beaten 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon all-purpose flour 3/4 teaspoon baking powder 3/4 teaspoon baking soda 1/2 teaspoon salt 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg 3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon raisins (optional) Directions Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease or paper-line muffin pans. In a small bowl soak oats in orange juice and water for 15 minutes. In a large bowl cream together butter or margarine and sugars. Beat in eggs and oat mixture. In a separate bowl, blend flour, baking powder, soda, salt, and nutmeg. Stir into batter. Stir in vanilla and raisins. Spoon batter into prepared muffin pans, filling 2/3 full.
