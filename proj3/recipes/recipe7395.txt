Recipe By:Judy and Amanda "My sisters and I always requested this cake for our birthdays. Now, my daughters ask for it for their birthdays!" Ingredients 1-2/3 cups all-purpose flour 1 cup and 2 tablespoons white sugar 1/3 cup and 1 teaspoon unsweetened cocoa powder 1/2 teaspoon salt 1 teaspoon baking soda 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract 1 cup and 2 tablespoons cold water 1 teaspoon distilled white vinegar 1/4 cup and 2 tablespoons vegetable oil Directions Mix all ingredients right in a 9 x 13 inch pan. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 35 minutes. Remove from oven and cool. Frost with your favorite frosting.
