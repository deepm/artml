Recipe By:Carol "Easy-to-make chocolate cake with a gooey caramel center--almost like a candy bar." Ingredients 3/8 (18.25 ounce) package German chocolate cake mix 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon semisweet chocolate chips 3/8 (5 ounce) can evaporated milk 6 ounces individually wrapped caramels 1/4 cup and 1 tablespoon butter 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon walnuts Directions Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease one 13x9 inch cake pan. Make cake mix according to package directions. Pour 1/2 of the batter into the prepared pan and bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 20 minutes. In a saucepan over medium low heat melt caramels, margarine, and milk. Pour over top of baked cake. Top with chocolate chips and 1/2 of the walnuts. Pour remaining cake batter over top and sprinkle with the remaining walnuts. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 20 to 25 minutes more.
