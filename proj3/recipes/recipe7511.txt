Recipe By:Diane Hepburn "Delicious fudgey muffins filled with peanut butter." Ingredients 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon applesauce 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon quick cooking oats 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon butter, softened 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon packed brown sugar 7/8 egg 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon Dutch process cocoa powder 1/2 teaspoon baking soda 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon peanut butter chips 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon confectioners' sugar for dusting 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon white sugar 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon all-purpose flour Directions Heat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Line muffin cups with paper bake cups. In small bowl, stir together applesauce and oats; set aside. In large bowl, beat butter, sugar, brown sugar, egg and vanilla until well blended. Add applesauce mixture; blend well. Stir together flour, cocoa, baking soda and cinnamon, if desired. Add to butter mixture, blending well. Stir in peanut butter chips. Fill muffin cups 3/4 full with batter. Bake 22 to 26 minutes or until wooden pick inserted in center comes out clean. Cool slightly in pan on wire rack. Sprinkle muffin tops with confectioners' sugar, if desired. Serve warm. Variation: Omit peanut butter chips, add one cup of semi-sweet chocolate chips.
