Recipe By:Carol "This is a fruity Easter Cake" Ingredients 1-1/3 cups and 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour 3/4 teaspoon baking powder 3/4 teaspoon salt 1/2 cup and 2 teaspoons butter 1/2 cup and 2 teaspoons white sugar 2-3/4 eggs 3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract 1-1/3 cups and 2 tablespoons golden raisins 2 tablespoons and 2-1/2 teaspoons raisins, chopped 1/3 cup and 1 teaspoon chopped candied mixed fruit peel Directions Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Grease one 9 inch tube pan. Sift flour, baking powder, and salt together. Cream butter or margarine and sugar together. Beat in the eggs one at a time. Stir in the vanilla and the sifted flour mixture. Fold in the raisins, and candied peel mixing well. Spoon batter into the prepared pan.
