Recipe By:Jennifer "Simple sourdough bread for the bread machine." Ingredients 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C) 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon sourdough starter 1-1/4 teaspoons salt 2-1/4 cups bread flour 1-1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast Directions Add all ingredients in order suggested by your manufacturer.
