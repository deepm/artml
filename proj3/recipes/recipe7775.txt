Recipe By:Carol "Maple flavored frosting that tastes great on the Maple Nut Cake or try it on a plain yellow or chocolate cake, yummy! Increase the amount of maple extract (gradually!) to suit your taste." Ingredients 1-2/3 cups confectioners' sugar 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon butter, softened 2 tablespoons and 1-1/2 teaspoons cream 1/2 teaspoon maple flavored extract Directions Beat the butter or margarine, confectioner's sugar, milk, and maple flavoring in a bowl until smooth. Add more milk or confectioner's sugar as need. Makes enough to frost one 8 or 9 inch round cake or 1 8x8 inch square cake.
