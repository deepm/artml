Recipe By:Janice Reesman "This recipe requires just a few ingredients to make an easy, quick, and good chocolate icing for cakes, brownies, or cookies." Ingredients 1-1/4 cups white sugar 3 tablespoons and 2-1/4 teaspoons unsweetened cocoa powder 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon milk 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon shortening 1-1/4 pinches salt 1-1/4 teaspoons vanilla extract Directions In a saucepan combine sugar, cocoa, milk, shortening, and salt. Bring mixture to a rolling boil, stirring constantly for 2 minutes. Remove from heat and add vanilla and continue beating until frosting starts to thicken slightly. The frosting will be real creamy.
