Recipe By:Carol "A rich luscious filling for Lane cake. It's made with coconut, pecans, raisins and candied cherries." Ingredients 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon butter, softened 1 cup and 1 tablespoon white sugar 6-3/4 egg yolks 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon water 3/4 teaspoon brandy flavoring 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon chopped pecans 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon raisins 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon candied cherries, chopped 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon flaked coconut Directions Put butter or margarine and the sugar into the top of a double boiler away from the heat. Beat together. Add egg yolks and beat well. Stir in water and brandy flavoring. Place over boiling water. Cook and stir until thickened. Add pecans, raisins, cherries and coconut.
