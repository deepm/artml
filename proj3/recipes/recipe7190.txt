Recipe By:Marian Collins "A crusty, chewy pizza crust! Our family's favorite! Try make two pizza crusts at once by removing the dough from the machine once it hits the rise cycle and place in a separate bowl. Now you are ready to begin the second crust!" Ingredients 1 cup and 1 tablespoon warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C) 1-2/3 cups all-purpose flour 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon semolina flour 1/2 teaspoon white sugar 3/4 teaspoon salt 1-3/4 teaspoons olive oil 1-3/4 teaspoons active dry yeast Directions Place all ingredients in the bread machine pan in the order suggested by the manufacturer. Select dough cycle. Remove after rise cycle and use with your favorite pizza recipe. ALL RIGHTS RESERVED © 2019 Allrecipes.com Printed From Allrecipes.com 3/29/2019
