Recipe By:Donna "Use a good sourdough starter, one you have tended to, for best flavor." Ingredients 2 cups bread flour 1 tablespoon and 3/4 teaspoon white sugar 1 teaspoon salt 3/8 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon warm milk 2-1/2 teaspoons margarine, softened 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons sourdough starter 3/8 extra large egg 1-1/4 teaspoons water 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons chopped onion Directions In a large bowl, combine 1 cup flour, sugar, salt, and dry yeast. Add milk and softened butter or margarine. Stir in starter. Mix in up to 3 3/4 cups flour gradually, you may need more depending on your climate. Turn dough out onto a floured surface, and knead for 8 to 10 minutes. Place in a greased bowl, turn once to oil surface, and cover. Allow to rise for 1 hour, or until doubled in volume. Punch down, and let rest 15 minutes. Shape into loaves. Place on a greased baking pan. Allow to rise for 1 hour, or until doubled. Brush egg wash over tops of loaves, and sprinkle with chopped onion. Bake at 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) for 30 minutes, or till done.
