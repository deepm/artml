Recipe By:Carol "This is a delicate white cake. Heavy cream is used instead of butter." Ingredients 2-1/4 eggs 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon white sugar 1 teaspoon vanilla extract 1-2/3 cups all-purpose flour 2-1/4 teaspoons baking powder 1 teaspoon salt 1 cup and 2 tablespoons heavy whipping cream Directions Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x9-inch square pan. Beat eggs in a small bowl until very thick. Add the sugar and the vanilla, beating well. Combine the flour, baking powder, and salt. Add the flour mixture in three parts, alternating with the whipping cream, mixing until combined. Pour the batter into the prepared pan.
