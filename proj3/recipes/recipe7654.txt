Recipe By:Carol "This is a very rich cake with Grand Marnier poured into it." Ingredients 1/3 cup and 1 teaspoon butter, softened 2/3 cup and 2 teaspoons white sugar 1/2 cup and 2 teaspoons packed brown sugar 1-1/2 eggs 3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract 2/3 cup and 2 teaspoons sour cream 1 tablespoon and 1-1/4 teaspoons orange zest 1-1/3 cups all-purpose flour 1/2 teaspoon baking powder 1/4 teaspoon baking soda 1/8 teaspoon salt 1/2 cup and 2 teaspoons chopped pecans 2 tablespoons and 2-1/2 teaspoons orange juice 1 tablespoon and 1-1/4 teaspoons brandy-based orange liqueur (such as Grand Marnier(R)) Directions Stir together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. In a large bowl, cream the butter or margarine, 3/4 cup granulated sugar, and brown sugar. Beat in eggs, then add vanilla, sour cream, and orange rind. Beat the flour mixture into the creamed mixture. Stir in the pecans. Pour the batter into a greased and floured tube pan. In a small bowl, mix together the remaining 1/4 cup sugar, the orange juice, and the liqueur. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 1 hour, or until it tests done with a toothpick.
