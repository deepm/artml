Recipe By:Carol "A creamy white icing that looks like whipped cream." Ingredients 2/3 cup milk 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon all-purpose flour 2/3 cup butter, softened 2/3 cup white sugar 3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract Directions Whisk milk into flour in small saucepan until smooth. Heat and stir until it boils and thickens. Cool thoroughly. Cream butter, sugar and vanilla in bowl until light and fluffy. Add thickened milk. Beat until mixture resembles whipped cream.
