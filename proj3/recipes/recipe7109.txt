Recipe By:Holly "Cheesy, applely bran muffins that make a great breakfast or afternoon snack." Ingredients 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon whole bran cereal 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon milk 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon peeled, cored and sliced apple 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon vegetable oil 7/8 egg 1-1/4 cups all-purpose flour 1 tablespoon baking powder 1/2 teaspoon salt 3/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon packed brown sugar 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon shredded Cheddar cheese Directions Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease one 12-cup muffin tin or line with paper liners. Combine the bran cereal, milk and apples. Mix well. Beat in the vegetable oil and egg. Combine the flour, baking powder, salt, cinnamon and brown sugar. Make a well in the center and stir in the wet mixture. Add the shredded cheese and mix enough until just moistened. Scoop the batter into the prepared pans. Bake at 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) for 15 to 20 minutes.
