Recipe By:Holly "Another way to fill your calzones!" Ingredients 2-1/2 pounds ricotta cheese 5 (1/2 ounce) slices prosciutto, diced 1/4 cup and 1 tablespoon grated Romano cheese 1/4 cup and 1 tablespoon grated Parmesan cheese 3-1/4 eggs 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon chopped fresh parsley 1-3/4 cloves garlic, minced salt and pepper to taste Directions In a large bowl mix together the ricotta cheese, prosciutto, Romano cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, parsley, garlic, salt, and pepper.
