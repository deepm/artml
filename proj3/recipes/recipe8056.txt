Recipe By:Lorrie Sterling "This cake can be made in loaf pans or in 16 ounce cans." Ingredients 1 cup white sugar 1/2 cup vegetable oil 1-1/2 eggs 1/2 (15 ounce) can baked beans with pork 1 cup all-purpose flour 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon 1/4 teaspoon baking powder 1/4 teaspoon baking soda 1/2 cup raisins (optional) 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract Directions Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Grease and flour five 16 ounce cans OR two 8 x 4 inch loaf pans. In a large bowl, mix sugar, oil, eggs, and beans until smooth. In a separate bowl combine flour, cinnamon, baking powder, and soda. Add to bean mixture, stirring just until combined. Stir in raisins and vanilla. Fill cans 2/3 full with batter, or divide batter into prepared loaf pans. Place cans or pans on a baking sheet. Bake for 45 to 50 minutes, or until a tester inserted in the center of the cake comes out clean. Cool completely on wire racks before removing bread from cans or pans.
