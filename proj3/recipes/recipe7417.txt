Recipe By:Virginia Godd "My mom made this cake while living in the Depression. It is so good, I hope this fills your needs and your memories." Ingredients 1-1/4 cups all-purpose flour 1/2 teaspoon baking soda 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons white sugar 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons mayonnaise 2 tablespoons and 3/4 teaspoon unsweetened cocoa powder 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons water 1 teaspoon baking powder 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract Directions Preheat oven to 350 degrees (175 degrees C). Lightly grease two 9 inch layer pans. Mix flour, baking soda, baking powder, cocoa, and sugar into a large bowl. Stir in mayonnaise. Gradually add water and vanilla and blend until smooth. Pour batter into prepared pans. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 30 minutes. Cool, remove from pans, and frost with your favorite chocolate frosting.
