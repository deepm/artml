Recipe By:Ron Evers "This bread stays fresh longer than any 'standard' white bread we have tried." Ingredients 2/3 cup water 5/8 extra large egg, beaten 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon dry milk powder 2 teaspoons white sugar 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon vegetable oil 1 teaspoon salt 2/3 cup bread flour 1-1/3 cups all-purpose flour 3/4 teaspoon active dry yeast Directions Place ingredients in order suggested by your manufacturer.
