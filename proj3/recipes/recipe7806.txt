Recipe By:Carol "This glaze is perfect for the Orange Chiffon Cake. This will give a thin coating or a drizzle over any cake especially a tube cake or bundt cake." Ingredients 1-2/3 cups confectioners' sugar 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons frozen orange juice concentrate, thawed Directions Mix all ingredients together well. Add more confectioner's sugar or orange juice to make the proper consistency for a barely pourable glaze.
