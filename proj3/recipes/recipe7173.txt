Recipe By:Dee "This batter can be stored in your refrigerator, unbaked for 6 weeks. Use only the amount you want each day. Mine never seems to last that long!" Ingredients 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon boiling water 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon whole bran cereal 2 tablespoons and 3/4 teaspoon shortening 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon white sugar 1/2 eggs 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon buttermilk 2/3 cup and 1 teaspoon all-purpose flour 3/4 teaspoon baking soda 1/8 teaspoon salt 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon whole bran cereal Directions In a large bowl, add boiling water to the first 6 cups of bran cereal, cool. Cream shortening or margarine, sugar, eggs, and buttermilk together. Add to bran mixture. Stir in flour, baking soda, and salt. Fold in the 12 cups of bran cereal. Stir only until moistened. Bake in a preheated 425 degree F (220 degree C) oven for 20 to 25 minutes.
