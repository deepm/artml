Recipe By:Nicole Ribecca "As Hawaiin as I am, this one is a shocker! But it tastes great, the beer gives it a delightful yeasty taste." Ingredients 7/8 (8 ounce) can pineapple chunks 2-1/4 cups and 1 tablespoon self-rising flour 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon white sugar 7/8 (12 fluid ounce) can or bottle beer Directions Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease one 9x5 inch baking pan. Combine the pineapple flour, sugar and beer. Do not overmix! Pour batter into the prepared pan. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for about 1 hour or until bread tests done.
