Recipe By:Diane "This bread is made with spelt flour. Spelt and lecithin can be purchased at health food stores." Ingredients 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon water 1-1/4 teaspoons vegetable oil 1-1/4 teaspoons honey 1/2 teaspoon lecithin 2-1/2 cups white spelt flour 2 tablespoons and 1-1/2 teaspoons dry milk powder 1-1/4 teaspoons salt 1-3/4 teaspoons active dry yeast Directions Place ingredients in order suggested by manufacturer. Bake on normal or basic cycle.
