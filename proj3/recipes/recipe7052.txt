Recipe By:Steve Lockhart "60% whole wheat bread that works every time!!" Ingredients 1 cup and 2 tablespoons water 1 tablespoon and 3/4 teaspoon dry milk powder 2-1/2 teaspoons molasses 2-1/2 teaspoons honey 2 tablespoons and 1-1/2 teaspoons margarine 3/4 teaspoon white sugar 3/4 teaspoon salt 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon rye flour 1-2/3 cups whole wheat flour 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon bread flour 2-1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast Directions Place the ingredients in the pan of the bread machine in the order recommended by the manufacturer. Select Whole Wheat setting, and Start.
