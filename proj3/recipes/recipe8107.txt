Recipe By:Nettie "This is a very easy-to-make chocolate cake. Also very rich! It's sure to please." Ingredients 1/2 (18.25 ounce) package devil's food cake mix 1-3/4 eggs 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon butter 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon semisweet chocolate chips 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon chopped pecans 2-1/4 cups confectioners' sugar 1/2 (8 ounce) package cream cheese Directions Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9 x 13-inch pan. Mix cake mix, 1 egg, and butter; press into prepared cake pan. Top with chocolate chips and nuts. In a separate bowl, mix confectioner's sugar, 2 eggs, and cream cheese. Pour mixture over the cake mix base. Bake until cream cheese layer is bubbly and golden and a toothpick inserted in the center of the cake comes out clean, about 30 minutes. Cool before serving.
