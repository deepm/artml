Recipe By:Mary Beth Guba "Sour cream brings the tangy to this quick and easy chocolate frosting recipe." Ingredients 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons semisweet chocolate chips 2 tablespoons and 1-1/2 teaspoons butter 1/4 cup and 1 tablespoon sour cream 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract 1/8 teaspoon salt 1-2/3 cups and 1 tablespoon confectioners' sugar Directions Melt chocolate and the butter together. Let cool and blend in the sour cream, vanilla, and salt. Gradually add the confectioner's sugar until the frosting is of spreading consistency, beat well.
