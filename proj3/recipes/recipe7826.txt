Recipe By:Carol "You don't have to make a Williamsburg orange cake to enjoy this creamy and buttery Williamsburg frosting made with orange liqueur." Ingredients 1/4 cup and 1 tablespoon butter, softened 2 tablespoons and 1-1/2 teaspoons orange liqueur 1-3/4 teaspoons orange zest 2-3/4 cups and 1 tablespoon confectioners' sugar Directions In a large bowl, blend butter with confectioners' sugar. Stir in orange liqueur and orange zest. Beat until light and fluffy. Use to frost cooled cake. ALL RIGHTS RESERVED Â© 2019 Allrecipes.com Printed From Allrecipes.com 3/30/2019
