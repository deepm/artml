Recipe By:Carol "You'll melt marshmallows in a double boiler on your way to making this great icing recipe and be glad you did it, too." Ingredients 1-3/4 egg whites 1-1/4 cups white sugar 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon cold water 1-1/4 teaspoons light corn syrup 3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon miniature marshmallows Directions Put egg whites, sugar, water and syrup in top of double boiler. Beat until mixed well. Place over rapidly boiling water. Beat constantly with electric beater while it cooks for 7 minutes or until it will stand in peaks when beater is raised. Remove from heat. Stir in marshmallows and vanilla. Beat until they melt.
