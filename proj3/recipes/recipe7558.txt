Recipe By:Carol "My mother used to make this as a base before making her marshmallow frosting on some of her cakes." Ingredients 1-3/4 egg whites 1-1/4 cups white sugar 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon cold water 1-1/4 teaspoons light corn syrup 3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract Directions Put egg whites, sugar, water and syrup in top of double boiler. Beat until mixed well. Place over rapidly boiling water. Beat constantly with electric beater while it cooks for 7 minutes or until it will stand in peaks when beater is raised. Remove from heat.
