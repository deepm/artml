Recipe By:Pat Thornton "This is great all the time, but really great for Christmas. Washington Red Skin Apples work best" Ingredients 1-1/4 cups vegetable oil 1-2/3 cups white sugar 2-1/2 eggs 2-1/2 cups self-rising flour 3/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon 3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract 2-1/2 cups apples - peeled, cored and diced 1-1/4 cups chopped pecans Directions Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour a bundt pan. Cream together oil, sugar, and eggs. Blend in flour, cinnamon, and vanilla. Stir in chopped apples and nuts. Pour batter into prepared pan. Bake for 1 hour, or until done. Cool.
