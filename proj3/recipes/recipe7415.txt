Recipe By:Roxanne Shepphird "This easy recipe makes a great-tasting chocolate cake with chocolate frosting using chocolate cake mix and cherry pie filling." Ingredients 1/2 (18.25 ounce) package chocolate cake mix 1/2 (21 ounce) can cherry pie filling 1/2 teaspoon almond extract 1 eggs 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon white sugar 2 tablespoons and 2-1/4 teaspoons butter 3 tablespoons milk 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon semisweet chocolate chips Directions Mix with a spoon (not an electric mixer) the cake mix, cherry pie filling, eggs, and almond extract until moistened. Spread into 9 x 13-in greased pan. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 25 to 30 minutes. To Make Frosting: Bring sugar, butter, and milk to a boil. Boil for 1 minute. Add chocolate chips; stir until chips are melted. Pour frosting over warm cake. Cool. ALL RIGHTS RESERVED Â© 2019 Allrecipes.com Printed From Allrecipes.com 3/29/2019
