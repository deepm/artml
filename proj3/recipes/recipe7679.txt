Recipe By:Stephanie "You can find the ingredient mentioned in the passage that follows it." Ingredients 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon butter, Judges 5:25 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon white sugar, Jeremiah 6:20 1-3/4 eggs, Isaiah 10:14 1 cup and 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour, 1 Kings 4:22 1 teaspoon baking powder, Luke 13:21 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon, 1 Kings 10:10 1/2 teaspoon ground mace, 1 Kings 10:10 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves, 1 Kings 10:10 1/4 teaspoon salt, Leviticus 2:13 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon water, Genesis 43:24 1-3/4 teaspoons honey, Proverbs 24:13 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon figs, 1 Samuel 30:11 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon raisins, 1 Samuel 30:11 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon almonds, Genesis 43:11 Directions Blend butter, sugar, spices and salt. Beat egg yolks and add. Sift in baking powder and flour, then add water and honey. Put fruit and nuts through food processor and flour well. Fold in stiffly beaten egg whites. Pour into a 13 x 9-inch pan and bake at 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) for one hour.
