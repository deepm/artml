Recipe By:LOIS JEAN ANDERSON "This is a moist spice cake with a 'secret' ingredient. They will never guess!" Ingredients 1/2 (14.75 ounce) can cream-style corn 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon packed brown sugar 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon white sugar 1-3/4 eggs 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon vegetable oil 1-3/4 teaspoons baking powder 1-1/4 cups all-purpose flour 1/2 teaspoon baking soda 1/2 teaspoon salt 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon raisins 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon chopped walnuts Directions Stir together flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt. In a mixing bowl, combine corn and sugars. Beat in eggs and oil till well blended. Mix flour mixture into batter. Stir in raisins and nuts. Pour batter into greased 9 x 13 inch pan.
