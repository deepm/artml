Recipe By:Corinne "Cream of coconut and sour cream help this cake recipe deliver a wonderfully moist coconut cake with a cream cheese icing." Ingredients 3/8 (18.25 ounce) package white cake mix 1-1/4 eggs 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons vegetable oil 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon cream of coconut 3/8 (8 ounce) container sour cream 3-1/2 ounces cream cheese 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract 2-1/2 teaspoons milk 1-2/3 cups confectioners' sugar 2-1/2 teaspoons flaked coconut Directions Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour one 9x13 inch pan. Combine the cake mix, eggs, vegetable oil, cream of coconut and the sour cream and mix well for 4 minutes. Pour batter into the prepared pan. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 35 to 40 minutes. Let cake cool then frost with cream cheese frosting and top with flaked coconut. To Make Frosting: Combine the softened cream cheese, vanilla, milk and confectioners' sugar. Mix until smooth and spread over cooled cake. Top with flaked coconut.
