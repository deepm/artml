Recipe By:Aaron Atkinson "A basic white bread recipe without the gluten. Easily made in your bread machine! Xanthan gum and the exotic flours are usually easily found in your local health food store." Ingredients 2-1/2 eggs 2-1/2 teaspoons cider vinegar 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon olive oil 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon honey 1-1/4 cups buttermilk, at room temperature 3/4 teaspoon salt 2-1/2 teaspoons xanthan gum 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon cornstarch 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon potato starch 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon soy flour 1-2/3 cups white rice flour 2-1/2 teaspoons active dry yeast Directions Place ingredients in the pan of the bread machine in the order recommended by the manufacturer. Select the sweet dough cycle. Five minutes into the cycle, check the consistency of the dough. Add additional rice flour or liquid if necessary. When bread is finished, let cool for 10 to 15 minutes before removing from pan. ALL RIGHTS RESERVED © 2019 Allrecipes.com Printed From Allrecipes.com 3/29/2019
