Recipe By:Kenneth Massey "This cake was passed on to my mother by my grandmother. It is one of my personal favorites. I hope it becomes one of yours." Ingredients 3/4 (18.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix 1/3 cup and 1 teaspoon melted butter 3/4 egg 6 ounces cream cheese 1-1/2 eggs 2-2/3 cups confectioners' sugar Directions Grease and flour one 13x9 inch pan. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Mix together cake mix, melted butter, and 1 egg. Press into prepared pan. In separate bowl mix cream cheese, 2 eggs, and confectioners' sugar.
