Recipe By:Sally "Easy, moist and yummy. Recipe may be cut in half for one cake." Ingredients 1-2/3 cups all-purpose flour 1 tablespoon and 1/4 teaspoon baking powder 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon shortening 1-2/3 cups white sugar 1-3/4 eggs 1-2/3 cups milk 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon butter 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon brown sugar 1-3/4 teaspoons ground cinnamon Directions In a large bowl, combine flour, baking powder and shortening, and mix until crumbly. Add sugar, eggs, and milk; mix thoroughly. Divide batter into two 8 inch round or square greased baking pans. Dot the top with 1/2 cup butter broken into pieces the size of lima beans. Sprinkle top with 1/4 cup brown sugar for each cake, sprinkle cinnamon on top. Bake at 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) for about 30 minutes.
