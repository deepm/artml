Recipe By:Carol "This is a tender angel cake with creamy almond filling." Ingredients 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon sifted cake flour 2 tablespoons and 1-1/2 teaspoons unsweetened cocoa powder 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon white sugar 5 egg whites 1/2 teaspoon cream of tartar 1/4 teaspoon salt 3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon white sugar 1 tablespoon and 1-1/4 teaspoons cornstarch 1/4 teaspoon salt 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon white sugar 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon milk 1-3/4 eggs 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons amaretto liqueur Directions Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Sift flour, cocoa, and 1/4 cup of the white sugar together. Beat egg whites with cream of tartar and salt until soft peaks form, gradually add 1/2 cup sugar beating until stiff and shiny, add vanilla. Sift dry mixture over beaten egg whites gradually and gently fold in. Spoon batter into one 9x5 inch ungreased loaf pan. Bake at 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) for 25 minutes or until done. Invert pan and cool completely. Remove cake and cut it into 3 layers. Spread almond filling between the layers and dust top with sifted confectioners' sugar. Makes about 10 servings. To Make Almond Filling: Combine the remaining 1/4 cup white sugar, cornstarch and salt in a saucepan. Mix the beaten eggs and milk together and stir them into the sugar mixture. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly until thick and bubbling. Cook for an additional 2 minutes. Remove from heat and add the almond liqueur and stir well.
