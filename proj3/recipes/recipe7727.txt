Recipe By:Carol "This is a moist and unusual cake. Great for summer." Ingredients 1 cup and 1 tablespoon butter 1-1/3 cups and 2 tablespoons white sugar 2-3/4 eggs 1/4 teaspoon salt 1-1/2 teaspoons baking soda 3/4 (20 ounce) can crushed pineapple, drained 2 tablespoons and 2-1/2 teaspoons unsweetened pineapple juice 3/4 (3.5 ounce) package flaked coconut 1 cup and 1 tablespoon chopped walnuts 1-1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract 3/4 (16 ounce) package graham crackers, crushed Directions Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and line with parchment paper one 10 inch tube pan. Cream butter or margarine, sugar, and eggs together; add salt, baking soda, crushed pineapple, and pineapple juice. Add coconut, walnuts, and vanilla. Mix in the graham cracker crumbs. Pour batter into the prepared pan. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 1 hour and 30 minutes.
