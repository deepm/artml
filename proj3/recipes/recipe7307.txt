Recipe By:tomg "This is an old Seattle recipe for a simple cake to turn overripe bananas into a delicious dessert." Ingredients 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon white sugar 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon unsalted butter 7/8 eggs 1-3/4 ripe bananas, mashed 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon all-purpose flour 1/2 teaspoon baking soda 1/4 teaspoon salt 1/4 cup and 1 tablespoon chopped walnuts 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract Directions Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease and flour one 9 x 13 inch cake pan. Cream together the butter or margarine and the sugar. Add eggs, bananas, flour, soda, salt, nuts, and vanilla. Mix thoroughly and pour batter into the prepared pan. Bake at 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) for 30 minutes. Cool and frost cake.
