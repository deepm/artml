Recipe By:Suzanne "Italian dessert cake. Not exactly like what you would get in an authentic Italian restaurant, but still good." Ingredients 1-1/4 cups mascarpone cheese 1-3/4 cups and 2 tablespoons heavy whipping cream 2 tablespoons and 1-1/2 teaspoons confectioners' sugar 1 tablespoon and 2-1/2 teaspoons coffee-flavored liqueur 1/2 teaspoon instant coffee granules 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract 5/8 sponge cake 2 tablespoons and 1-1/2 teaspoons unsweetened cocoa powder Directions Beat mascarpone cheese, cream, confectioners' sugar, coffee liqueur, instant coffee, and vanilla extract together in a bowl using an electric mixer until stiff peaks form. Cut sponge cake in half horizontally, creating a top and bottom layer. Spread cream mixture on bottom layer and replace top half of cake; refrigerate for 1 hour. Dust top of cake with cocoa powder.
