Recipe By:Rene "Easy to make and delicious too!" Ingredients 1/2 (18.25 ounce) package chocolate cake mix 2-1/4 cups part-skim ricotta cheese 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon white sugar 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract 2-1/4 eggs 1/2 (3.9 ounce) package instant chocolate pudding mix 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon milk 1/2 (12 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed Directions Prepare cake mix as directed on box. Pour batter into 9 x 13 x 2 inch greased baking dish. Set aside. Combine ricotta cheese, sugar, vanilla, and eggs. Blend well. Spread mixture evenly over the top of the cake batter. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 75 minutes if using a glass baking dish, 90 minutes if using a metal pan. Blend pudding mix and milk until thickened. Blend in whipped topping. Spread over cooled cake.
