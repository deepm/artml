Recipe By:L. Dart "This cake can be frozen for up to 6 months. To make sour milk, mix 1/2 cup milk with 2 teaspoons lemon juice and let stand 10 minutes." Ingredients 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon butter 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon vegetable oil 3/4 cup and 3 tablespoons and 2 teaspoons white sugar 1 eggs 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon sour milk 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract 1 cup and 2 tablespoons sifted all-purpose flour 1/2 teaspoon baking soda 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves 1/4 teaspoon salt 2 tablespoons and 3/4 teaspoon unsweetened cocoa powder 1-1/3 cups and 1 tablespoon grated zucchini 2 tablespoons and 3/4 teaspoon ground walnuts 2 tablespoons and 3/4 teaspoon semisweet chocolate chips Directions Cream together butter or margarine, vegetable oil and sugar. Add eggs, sour milk, and vanilla. Blend well. In a separate bowl, sift dry ingredients together. Blend dry ingredients into the butter mixture. Add grated zucchini, and mix well. Spread in a greased floured 9 x 13 inch pan. Sprinkle walnuts and chocolate chips on top. Bake in a preheated 325 degrees F (165 degrees C) oven for 45 minutes.
