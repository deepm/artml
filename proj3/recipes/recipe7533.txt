Recipe By:JJOHN32 "A delicious smooth cheesecake served with or without a fruit topping. I like to make this cheesecake a day or so before I wish to serve it. It always seems to taste better with the extra setting time." Ingredients 1-1/3 cups and 2 tablespoons finely ground graham cracker crumbs 1/4 cup and 1 tablespoon finely chopped walnuts 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon melted butter 2-1/2 eggs 1-3/4 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon white sugar 1/4 teaspoon salt 1-3/4 teaspoons vanilla extract 1/2 teaspoon almond extract 2-1/2 cups sour cream Directions Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Mix graham cracker crumbs, walnuts, cinnamon and melted butter together. Press on bottom and sides of a 9 inch springform pan. Combine 3 well-beaten eggs, cream cheese, sugar, salt, vanilla and almond extract. Beat until smooth. Blend in the sour cream and pour into the crumb crust. Bake at 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) for about 35 minutes or just till set. Cool. Chill well, about 4 or 5 hours. Filling will be soft.
