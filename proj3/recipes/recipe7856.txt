Recipe By:Nina "This is a great last-minute recipe. It takes minutes to make and is light and delicious. If in a rush, you can serve it right away, but it's best if refrigerated for 24 hours to allow the flavors to blend." Ingredients 7/8 (10 inch) angel food cake 7/8 (12 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed 5 (1.4 ounce) bars chocolate covered English toffee Directions Split angel food cake in half horizontally. Stir together the whipped topping and the crushed toffee bars. Use to fill and frost cake. Cover and refrigerate cake.
