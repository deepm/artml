Recipe By:Amy Parsons "This chocolate cake tastes rich enough to be served without frosting. Simply dust top with powdered sugar." Ingredients 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon all-purpose flour 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon unsweetened cocoa powder 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon white sugar 3/4 teaspoon baking soda 1/2 teaspoon baking powder 1/8 teaspoon salt 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon nonfat milk 7/8 (4 ounce) jar pureed prunes baby food 3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract 1-3/4 egg whites 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon strong brewed coffee Directions Spray two 9 inch round cake pans with nonstick spray. Preheat oven to 350 degrees C (175 degrees C). In large mixing bowl, sift together flour, cocoa, sugar, baking soda, baking powder, and salt. In medium bowl, stir together milk, prunes, vanilla, egg whites or egg mixture, and coffee. Pour moist ingredients into dry, blending well. Divide batter equally between pans.
