Recipe By:Sandra Bennett "This frosting recipe quickly makes a rich, creamy, and dark chocolate icing with just bittersweet chocolate, brandy, butter, and heavy cream." Ingredients 7 (1 ounce) squares bittersweet chocolate 1/2 cup heavy cream 3 tablespoons butter 3 tablespoons brandy Directions Melt chocolate, cream, butter or margarine, and brandy together until smooth.
