Recipe By:Tammy "This frosting can be used on any yellow or white layer cake. It's very good even on a box mix." Ingredients 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon water 2 tablespoons and 1-1/2 teaspoons cornstarch 2-1/2 egg yolks 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon lemon juice 1-1/4 cups white sugar 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon butter Directions Combine sugar and cornstarch in heavy saucepan add water and stir until heated, set aside. Beat egg yolks and add lemon juice, stir in small amount of sugar mixture to warm yolks. Combine yolk and sugar mixtures, add butter and return to heat. Bring to a rolling boil, stirring constantly until thickened. Mixture will resemble pudding. Cool slightly and spread on cooled cake. Makes enough to frost and fill one 8 inch four layer cake.
