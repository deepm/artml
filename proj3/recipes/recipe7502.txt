Recipe By:Carole Zee "This chocolate cake recipe features the surprise additions of cola and marshmallows to the batter." Ingredients 2/3 cup and 2 teaspoons butter 2 tablespoons and 2-1/2 teaspoons unsweetened cocoa powder 2/3 cup and 2 teaspoons cola-flavored carbonated beverage 1-1/3 cups and 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour 1-1/3 cups and 2 tablespoons white sugar 3/4 teaspoon baking soda 1/3 cup and 1 teaspoon buttermilk 1-1/2 eggs 3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract 1 cup and 1 tablespoon miniature marshmallows Directions Heat to boiling: butter, cocoa and cola drink. Remove from heat and add flour, sugar, baking soda and mix gently. Stir in buttermilk, eggs, vanilla and marshmallows. Pour into greased 13 x 9-inch pan.
