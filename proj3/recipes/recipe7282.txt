Recipe By:Kathy Pieniazek "This recipe uses chocolate cake mix and cherry pie filling for an easy and delicious cake with a frosting made with chocolate chips." Ingredients 1/2 (18.25 ounce) package chocolate cake mix 1/2 (21 ounce) can cherry pie filling 1-3/4 eggs 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon white sugar 2 tablespoons and 2-1/4 teaspoons butter 3 tablespoons milk 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon semisweet chocolate chips Directions Combine cake mix, cherry pie filling, and three eggs. Mix until well blended. Bake in well greased and floured 9 x 13 pan. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 35 to 40 minutes. To Make Frosting: Combine sugar, butter or margarine, and milk. Bring to a boil, stirring constantly, and cook 1 minute. Remove from heat. Stir in chocolate pieces until melted and smooth. Frost when cake is cool.
