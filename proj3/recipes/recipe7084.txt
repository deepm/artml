Recipe By:Sharon Spencer "Irish Soda Bread for the bread machine. This recipe makes a wonderfully tasty loaf." Ingredients 1 cup warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C) 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon margarine 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon white sugar 3/4 teaspoon salt 2-3/4 cups and 1 tablespoon bread flour 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon dry milk powder 2 teaspoons caraway seed 1-1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast 1/4 cup and 3 tablespoons raisins Directions Add ingredients in order suggested by your manufacturer. Select fruit bread or basic bread setting. Add raisins when indicated by your manufacturer. ALL RIGHTS RESERVED © 2019 Allrecipes.com Printed From Allrecipes.com 3/29/2019
