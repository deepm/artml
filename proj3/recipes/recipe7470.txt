Recipe By:ZOOKIE "We make this, and top it with Broiled Coconut Topping! Really easy!" Ingredients Cake: 1 cup and 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour 1 teaspoon baking powder 2-1/4 eggs 1 cup and 2 tablespoons white sugar 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon milk 2 tablespoons and 3/4 teaspoon butter Topping: 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon packed brown sugar 2 tablespoons and 3/4 teaspoon butter, room temperature 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon flaked coconut 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon chopped walnuts 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon milk Directions Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9-inch square pan. In a large mixing bowl, beat eggs for 4 minutes with electric mixer. Gradually add sugar; beat until light and fluffy, 4 to 5 minutes. Whisk together the flour and baking powder, and add to egg mixture until just combined. In a saucepan, heat 1/2 cup milk and 2 tablespoons butter until butter is melted. Gradually pour into the batter, mixing constantly. Pour into prepared pan. Bake in preheated oven until cake is golden and a toothpick inserted in the center of the cake comes out clean, 20 to 25 minutes. Remove cake from oven; preheat oven's broiler. Beat 1/4 cup brown sugar and 2 tablespoons butter until combined. Stir in milk to desired consistency. Stir in coconut and nuts. Spread over warm cake.
