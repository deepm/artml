Recipe By:Mary Kaye "This is a soft waffle. A childhood favorite." Ingredients 3-1/3 cups all-purpose flour 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons baking powder 1-3/4 teaspoons salt 1/4 cup and 1 tablespoon white sugar 6-3/4 egg yolks 6-3/4 egg whites 2 cups and 1 tablespoon milk 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon vegetable oil Directions In a large bowl sift together the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar. Make a well in the center of the bowl. In a separate bowl, mix together the egg yolks, milk, and oil. Pour into well of flour mixture and stir well. Stir just until ingredients are moistened.
