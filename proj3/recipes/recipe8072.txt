Recipe By:Jacqui "A delicious way to use my favorite fruit. If you don't have buttermilk, you may substitute sour milk. Stir 1 tablespoon of vinegar or lemon juice together with 1 cup of milk and let stand for 10 minutes." Ingredients 1-1/4 cups all-purpose flour 3/4 teaspoon baking soda 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon butter 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon white sugar 1-3/4 eggs 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon buttermilk 3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon mango puree 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons chopped walnuts Directions Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease a 9 x 5 inch loaf pan. Cream butter or margarine and sugar till light and fluffy. Add eggs and beat well. Mix together flour and baking soda; blend into creamed mixture. Fold in buttermilk, vanilla, mango puree, and chopped nuts. Pour batter into prepared pan. Bake for 40 to 50 minutes, or until done.
