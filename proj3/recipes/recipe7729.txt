Recipe By:JGP "Perfect for a hot summer's day. Nothing quite hits the spot like ice cream." Ingredients 6-2/3 cups vanilla ice cream 3/8 (20 ounce) package chocolate sandwich cookies with creme filling 3/8 (12 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed Directions Soften ice cream. Crush cookies by placing them in a large zip lock bag and rolling over them with a rolling pin. Mix softened ice cream and crushed cookies together. Fold in the whipped topping and pour into one 9x13 inch glass pan. Freeze for 2 hours then serve.
