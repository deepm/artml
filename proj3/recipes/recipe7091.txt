Recipe By:Barbara "Moist banana bread for the bread machine. All the flavor less of the work!" Ingredients 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C) 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons mashed bananas 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons margarine, softened 7/8 egg 2-2/3 cups and 1 tablespoon bread flour 2 tablespoons and 1-1/2 teaspoons white sugar 1 teaspoon salt 2-1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon chopped macadamia nuts Directions Place ingredient in bread machine in order suggested by your manufacturer. Select sweet bread setting and light crust. Add macadamia nuts when indicated by your manufacturer. ALL RIGHTS RESERVED © 2019 Allrecipes.com Printed From Allrecipes.com 3/29/2019
