Recipe By:Joyce "This is a great cake that can be put together in one bowl quickly when company is coming. It's delicious when served warm with a dollop of whipped cream on top. Use any flavor pie filling." Ingredients 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon white sugar 1 cup and 2 tablespoons sifted all-purpose flour 1/2 teaspoon salt 1/2 teaspoon baking soda 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon vegetable oil 1-3/4 eggs, beaten 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon chopped walnuts (optional) 1/2 (21 ounce) can cherry pie filling Directions Grease and flour a 9 x 13 inch pan. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C ). Toss sugar, sifted flour, salt, soda, and cinnamon together in a large bowl. Fold in oil, eggs, chopped nuts, and pie filling. Pour batter into prepared pans. Bake for 45 to 50 minutes, or until cake tests done.
