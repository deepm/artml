Recipe By:Barbara "Excellent for the holidays. Better baked ahead of time. DELICIOUS flavor gets better with age." Ingredients 2-1/2 cups all-purpose flour 1/4 teaspoon salt 2/3 cup and 2 teaspoons butter 1-1/3 cups and 2 tablespoons white sugar 2-3/4 eggs 3/4 teaspoon baking soda 1/3 cup and 1 teaspoon buttermilk 11 ounces orange-flavored fruit slice jelly candies, chopped 2/3 cup and 2 teaspoons chopped dates 2 cups and 1 tablespoon chopped walnuts 2/3 cup and 2 teaspoons flaked coconut 2/3 cup and 2 teaspoons orange juice 1-1/3 cups and 2 tablespoons confectioners' sugar Directions Sift flour and salt together. Cream butter or margarine and sugar well. Add eggs, flour mixture, soda, and buttermilk; mix well. Fold in orange slices, dates, nuts, and coconut. Pour batter into a greased and floured tube pan. Bake at 300 degrees F (150 degrees C) for 1 3/4 to 2 hours. Remove cake from oven. Mix together juice and confectioner's sugar; pour over hot cake while still in pan. Cool cake in pan for 20 minutes. Turn out onto cake plate. Cool completely.
