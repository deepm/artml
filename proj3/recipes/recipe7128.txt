Recipe By:Jennifer "This was given to me by a friend who also uses a bread machine." Ingredients 1 cup warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C) 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon dry milk powder 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon vegetable oil 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon molasses 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon honey 1 teaspoon salt 1-1/2 cups whole wheat flour 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon bread flour 2/3 cup whole bran cereal 1-1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast Directions Place ingredients in order indicated by your manufacturer.
