Recipe By:Donna Johnsen "It gives you an extremely unique taste with a lot of moisture, and goes very well with a scoop of vanilla ice cream." Ingredients 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons vegetable oil 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons dry sherry 3-1/4 eggs 3/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg 7/8 (18.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon confectioners' sugar for dusting Directions Prepare a bundt pan by oiling the sides. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Blend all ingredients until well moistened. Pour batter into pan. Bake for 40 minutes, or until done. Remove cake from oven, and cool. Dust top with confectioners' sugar.
