Recipe By:Paula Seymour "Want to make fruit cake for the holidays but would rather a no-bake option? Try this version with almonds, marshmallows, dates, and raisins." Ingredients 1/2 pound sweetened condensed milk 1 cup and 2 tablespoons raisins 1 cup and 2 tablespoons chopped almonds 1 cup and 2 tablespoons chopped marshmallows 1 cup and 2 tablespoons candied pineapple chunks 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg 1/4 teaspoon ground allspice 1 cup and 2 tablespoons dates, pitted and chopped 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon dried currants 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon chopped walnuts 2-1/4 cups graham cracker crumbs 2 tablespoons and 3/4 teaspoon chopped candied cherries 1/4 teaspoon salt 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves Directions Grease one 9x13 inch pan. Combine all the ingredients and mix well. Spread batter into the prepared pan. Let cake stand in a cool place and age for at least one week before serving.
