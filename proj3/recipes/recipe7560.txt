Recipe By:Carol "This maple-flavored dessert icing with egg whites, brown sugar, and corn syrup is of a light butterscotch color." Ingredients 1-3/4 egg whites 1-1/4 cups packed brown sugar 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon cold water 1-1/4 teaspoons light corn syrup 3/4 teaspoon maple flavored extract Directions Put egg whites, brown sugar, water and syrup in top of double boiler. Beat until mixed well. Place over rapidly boiling water. Beat constantly with electric beater while it cooks for 7 minutes or until it will stand in peaks when beater is raised. Remove from heat.
