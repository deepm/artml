Recipe By:v monte "This recipe is very moist and rich. I have also made it in loaf pans, and given it away for holidays." Ingredients 2-1/2 eggs 1-2/3 cups white sugar 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon butter, softened 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon water 1-3/4 cups and 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons unsweetened cocoa powder 3/4 teaspoon baking soda 3/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon 3/4 teaspoon ground allspice 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon semisweet chocolate chips 1-2/3 cups grated apple 2-1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract Directions Cream together until fluffy the eggs, sugar, butter or margarine, and water. In another bowl, sift together the flour, cocoa, baking soda, and spices. Add dry ingredients to creamed mixture and mix well. Fold in chocolate chips, apple, and vanilla extract. Stir until these ingredients are evenly distributed. Spoon into greased, floured 10 inch bundt or loose bottom tube pan.
