Recipe By:Carol "Use strong brewed coffee in this quick and easy cake icing recipe with butter and vanilla extract." Ingredients 3-1/3 cups confectioners' sugar 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon butter, softened 1/4 cup and 1 tablespoon strong brewed coffee 1-3/4 teaspoons vanilla extract Directions Beat together confectioners' sugar, butter or margarine, coffee, and vanilla until smooth.
