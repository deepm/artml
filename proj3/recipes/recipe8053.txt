Recipe By:Nicole Cama "This cake recipe makes a frosted applesauce spice cake with a lemon-flavored frosting." Ingredients 1 cup and 1 tablespoon cake flour 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon white sugar 1/2 teaspoon salt 1/8 teaspoon baking powder 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves 1/4 teaspoon ground allspice 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons applesauce 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon water 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon shortening 7/8 eggs 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon raisins 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon chopped almonds 7/8 egg whites 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon packed brown sugar 1-1/4 teaspoons lemon juice 1/4 cup and 1 tablespoon finely chopped walnuts Directions Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour one 9x13 inch pan. Beat flour, white sugar, salt, baking powder, cinnamon, cloves, allspice, applesauce, water, shortening, 2 eggs, raisins, and 1/2 cup chopped almonds with a mixer on low, scraping the bowl constantly, 30 seconds. Beat on high speed, scraping bowl occasionally, 3 minutes. Pour into prepared pan. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) until wooden pick inserted in center comes out clean, 60-65 minutes. To Make Frosting: Just before cake is removed from the oven, beat egg whites until foamy. Beat brown sugar and lemon juice gradually into egg whites until stiff. Carefully spread over HOT cake.
