Recipe By:Cathy "Pineapple, musts, cranberries, and coconut combine in this moist fruitcake using artificial sweetener in place of sugar." Ingredients 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon chopped golden raisins 1/2 teaspoon salt 1-3/4 cups and 1 tablespoon chopped walnuts 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon chopped cranberries 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon unsweetened crushed pineapple 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon grated lemon peel 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon flaked coconut 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon orange juice 6-3/4 packets artificial sweetener 3/4 teaspoon ground mace 3/4 teaspoon ground allspice 3/4 teaspoon baking soda 1-1/4 cups all-purpose flour Directions Mix sugar substitute with orange juice. Pour over chopped cranberries. Soak 1 hour, mixing often. Mix raisins, nuts, coconut, lemon rind in flour until well coated. Add cranberries and orange juice mixture. Sprinkle baking soda over mixture, and mix. Mix in spices. Fold in crushed pineapple. Pour batter into a greased and floured 9 inch loaf pan. Bake at 325 degrees F (165 degrees C) for 40 minutes. Cool.
