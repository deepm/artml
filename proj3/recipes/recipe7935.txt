Recipe By:JJOHN32 "Can be made any time of the year, thanks to frozen blueberries." Ingredients 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon graham cracker crumbs 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons white sugar 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon melted butter 1-3/4 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon sour cream 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons white sugar 3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons all-purpose flour 3-1/4 eggs 1-2/3 cups frozen blueberries, dry pack 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon blueberry jelly Directions Combine crumbs, 2 tablespoons sugar and butter. Pat mixture into the bottom of a 9 inch springform pan. Mash cream cheese until soft and creamy. Gradually beat in sour cream, 3/4 cup sugar, vanilla and flour. Beat in eggs one at a time. Pour mixture into crumb-lined pan. Bake in a preheated 325 degree F (165 degrees C) oven for 1 hour or until firm to the touch. Cool and then remove cake from pan by loosening edges with a knife.
