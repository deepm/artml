Recipe By:Kathy Nowell "Has a texture like angel food cake." Ingredients 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C) 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon cottage cheese, room temperature 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons vegetable oil 7/8 egg 2-1/2 cups all-purpose flour 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons white sugar 1/4 teaspoon baking soda 3/4 teaspoon salt 1-1/2 teaspoons active dry yeast Directions Add the ingredients to the pan of your bread machine in the order suggested by the manufacturer, and Start. You can use more bread flour if the dough seems too sticky. ALL RIGHTS RESERVED © 2019 Allrecipes.com Printed From Allrecipes.com 3/29/2019
