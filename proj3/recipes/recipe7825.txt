Recipe By:Carol "This is a lovely orange cake for when company calls." Ingredients 1-1/2 cups cake flour 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon white sugar 3/4 teaspoon baking soda 1/2 teaspoon salt 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon butter, softened 2 tablespoons and 3/4 teaspoon shortening 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon buttermilk 1-3/4 eggs 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon golden raisins, chopped 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon chopped walnuts 1-3/4 teaspoons orange zest 3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract 1 recipe Williamsburg Butter Frosting Directions Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour a 9x13 inch pan, two 9-inch round cake pans, or three 8-inch round cake pans. In a large bowl, combine cake flour, sugar, baking soda and salt. Mix in butter, shortening, buttermilk, eggs, raisins, nuts, orange zest and vanilla. Beat with an electric mixer for 3 minutes on high speed. Pour batter into prepared pan. Bake in preheated oven until a toothpick inserted in center of cake comes out clean. Bake 9x13 inch pan 45 to 50 minutes, or layers 30 to 35 minutes.
