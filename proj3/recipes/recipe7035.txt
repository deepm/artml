Recipe By:Mary "I have been looking for a good Russian Black Bread recipe. This one is the closest I have come. The vinegar adds a bit of a bite, but believe me, with cheese it is marvelous." Ingredients 1-1/4 cups water 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons cider vinegar 2 cups and 1 tablespoon bread flour 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon rye flour 3/4 teaspoon salt 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons margarine 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons dark corn syrup 2-1/2 teaspoons brown sugar 2 tablespoons and 1-1/2 teaspoons unsweetened cocoa powder 3/4 teaspoon instant coffee granules 2-1/2 teaspoons caraway seed 1/4 teaspoon fennel seed (optional) 1-3/4 teaspoons active dry yeast Directions Place ingredients into the bread machine in order suggested by the manufacturer. Use the whole wheat, regular crust setting. After the baking cycle ends, remove bread from pan, place on a cake rack, and allow to cool for 1 hour before slicing.
