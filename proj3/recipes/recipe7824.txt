Recipe By:Carol "This recipe makes a delicious lemon-flavored pound cake with chopped pecans and a simple lemon glaze." Ingredients 2/3 cup and 2 teaspoons butter 1-1/3 cups and 2 tablespoons confectioners' sugar 2-1/4 eggs 1 cup and 1 tablespoon cake flour 3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract 2-1/4 teaspoons lemon zest 1/3 cup and 1 teaspoon chopped pecans 2/3 cup and 2 teaspoons sifted confectioners' sugar 1 tablespoon and 1-1/4 teaspoons fresh lemon juice Directions In a large bowl, cream butter and 2 cups confectioners' sugar until fluffy. Beat in eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in flour and vanilla, then pecans and lemon peel. Turn batter into greased 9 inch tube pan. Bake at 325 degrees F (165 degrees C) for 40 to 45 minutes, or until done. Cool. Remove cake from pan, and invert onto serving plate.
