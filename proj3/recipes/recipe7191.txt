Recipe By:GINNY M. "Hearty and delicious, this bread tastes and smells great." Ingredients 2/3 cup water 2 teaspoons vegetable oil 1/3 cup frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained 2 cups all-purpose flour 1/3 cup shredded Cheddar cheese 3/4 teaspoon salt 2 teaspoons white sugar 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper 1-3/4 teaspoons active dry yeast Directions Place all ingredients in bread machine pan in the order recommended by the manufacturer. Select white bread cycle.
