Recipe By:Andrea "This super recipe is easy to make, using yellow cake mix, canned cherries, and crushed pineapple to make a dump cake." Ingredients 3/8 (18.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix 3/8 (15 ounce) can crushed pineapple 3/8 (16.5 ounce) can pitted dark sweet cherries 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon butter Directions Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Dump pineapple and cherries into one 9x13 inch pan and mix together. Top with cake mix and slice butter or margarine over mix. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 45 minutes or until brown and bubbling. Remove and serve warm or cold.
