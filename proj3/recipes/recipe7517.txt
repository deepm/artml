Recipe By:Joan "Our family's favorite chocolate cake! Especially when you add the fudge frosting!" Ingredients 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon butter 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon shortening 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon water 2 tablespoons and 3/4 teaspoon unsweetened cocoa powder 1 cup and 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour 1 cup and 2 tablespoons white sugar 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon milk 1-3/4 teaspoons distilled white vinegar 1 eggs 1/2 teaspoon baking soda 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon butter 2 tablespoons and 3/4 teaspoon milk 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons unsweetened cocoa powder 1-2/3 cups sifted confectioners' sugar 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract Directions Bring to a boil the 1/2 cup butter or margarine, 1/2 cup shortening, water and 1/4 cup cocoa. Remove from heat and add flour, sugar, 1/2 cup milk and vinegar. Mix well. Then add: eggs, baking soda and 1 teaspoon vanilla. Mix well until lumps are gone. Pour into greased 9 x 13 inch pan. Bake at 400 degrees F (205 degrees C) for 20 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean. To Make Fudge Frosting: Combine in saucepan , 6 tablespoon butter or oleo, 4 tablespoons milk and cocoa. Bring to rolling boil. Remove from heat and add 3 cups confectioners' sugar and 1 teaspoon vanilla. Beat until smooth. Spread on cooled cake.
