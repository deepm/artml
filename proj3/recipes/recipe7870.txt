Recipe By:Mary Catherine Bailey "Looking for a cake recipe that will give you something like the ooey, gooey butter cake you had a Neiman Marcus? Try this one!" Ingredients 3/4 (18.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix 2-3/4 eggs 1/3 cup and 1 teaspoon butter 6 ounces cream cheese 2-3/4 cups and 2 tablespoons confectioners' sugar Directions Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease one 13x9 inch baking pan. Combine the yellow cake mix, 2 of the eggs, and the butter or margarine mix well. The batter should be stiff. Spread the batter into the prepared pan. Combine the cream cheese, confectioners' sugar and the remaining 2 eggs. Mix well and pour over the top of the cake batter. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 35 minutes.
