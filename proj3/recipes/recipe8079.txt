Recipe By:Barbara "Make this recipe for a delicious and moist Italian creme layer cake topped with coconut and pecans for a delicious and elegant dessert." Ingredients 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon buttermilk 3/4 teaspoon baking soda 1-2/3 cups white sugar 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon butter 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon vegetable oil 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon shortening 3-1/4 egg yolks 3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract 3-1/4 egg whites 1-2/3 cups all-purpose flour 7/8 (3.5 ounce) package flaked coconut 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon chopped pecans 7/8 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon margarine, softened 3-1/3 cups confectioners' sugar 3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon chopped pecans Directions Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Grease three 9 inch, round cake pans. Combine soda and buttermilk, and let stand a few minutes. In a large bowl, cream sugar, 1/2 cup butter, 1/2 cup oil and shortening. Add egg yolks one at a time, beating well after each addition. Mix buttermilk mixture alternately with flour into creamed mixture. Stir in 1 teaspoon vanilla. In a large glass or metal mixing bowl, beat egg whites until stiff peaks form. Fold 1/3 of the whites into the batter, then quickly fold in remaining whites until no streaks remain. Gently stir in 1 cup pecans and coconut. Pour batter into prepared pans. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 to 30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean. Allow to cool. To make the Cream Cheese Frosting: Beat together cream cheese, 1/2 cup butter or margarine, 1 teaspoon vanilla, and confectioners' sugar. Stir in 1 cup chopped pecans.
