Recipe By:Glenda "Great cake for the holidays." Ingredients 1-2/3 cups all-purpose flour 3/4 teaspoon baking soda 1/4 teaspoon salt 3/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon chopped pecans (optional) 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon raisins (optional) 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon shortening 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon white sugar 2-1/2 eggs 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon blackberry preserves 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons buttermilk 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon butter 7 ounces cream cheese 13 ounces confectioners' sugar 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract Directions Grease and flour two 8 or 9 inch round pans. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Mix flour with soda, salt, cinnamon, and cloves. Add nuts and raisins to dry ingredients if using. In a separate bowl, cream shortening and sugar. Add eggs one at a time, beating continuously. Add jam. Add flour mixture alternately with milk. Pour batter into prepared pans. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 35 minutes. Cool, and frost with Cream Cheese Frosting. To make Cream Cheese Frosting: mix together until creamy 1/4 cup butter, cream cheese, confectioners' sugar, and vanilla.
