Recipe By:Adora Marchi "You might think this cake recipe is a bit wacky because it has vinegar but no eggs, but only until you're enjoying a rich chocolate cake." Ingredients 2 cups and 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour 1-1/3 cups and 2 tablespoons white sugar 1-1/2 teaspoons baking soda 1 teaspoon salt 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon unsweetened cocoa powder 1/4 cup and 3 tablespoons margarine, melted 1 tablespoon and 1-1/4 teaspoons distilled white vinegar 1-1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract 1-1/3 cups and 2 tablespoons water 2-3/4 cups and 2 tablespoons confectioners' sugar 2 tablespoons and 1/2 teaspoon unsweetened cocoa powder 1/3 cup and 1 teaspoon margarine 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon water Directions Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Sift the flour, white sugar, baking soda, salt and 6 tablespoons of the cocoa into a 9 x 13 inch baking pan. Drizzle melted margarine and the vinegar over the flour mixture. Add the vanilla and cold water and stir well to remove lumps. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 35 minutes. Spread with chocolate frosting. To Make Chocolate Frosting: Combine the confectioner's sugar, 6 tablespoons water, 3 tablespoons cocoa, and 1/2 cup of the margarine. Heat until melted and stir until smooth add vanilla and nuts if desired, and spread over cake.
