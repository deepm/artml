Recipe By:Linda "A rich eggy bread, made easier by preparing the dough in the bread machine." Ingredients 3 tablespoons and 1-3/4 teaspoons warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C) 2 eggs 1-1/4 egg yolks 1/2 cup butter, softened 2-1/4 cups all-purpose flour 2 tablespoons and 2 teaspoons white sugar 1/4 teaspoon active dry yeast 5/8 egg white 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon water Directions Place the first seven ingredients in bread machine in order suggested by your manufacturer. Select dough cycle. Turn finished dough out onto a lightly floured board and knead 5 to 10 times. Separate into 2 or 3 pieces. Roll with hands into strips. Braid or twist strips together. Place onto a parchment lined baking sheet. Set aside to rise in a warm place until doubled in size. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Whisk together 1 egg white and the water. Brush onto the top of the loaf. Bake in the preheated oven until deep golden brown, about 20 minutes. ALL RIGHTS RESERVED © 2019 Allrecipes.com Printed From Allrecipes.com 3/29/2019
