Recipe By:Carol "A lovely cake for mother's day or any special occasion." Ingredients 2-1/2 cups cake flour 1-2/3 cups white sugar 3/4 teaspoon salt 1 tablespoon and 1-1/4 teaspoons baking powder 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons shortening 1-1/4 cups milk 3-1/4 eggs 1-1/4 teaspoons vanilla extract 2-1/2 cups white sugar 2-1/2 teaspoons light corn syrup 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon boiling water 2-1/2 egg whites 3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon flaked coconut Directions Mix flour, 2 cups sugar, salt, and baking powder; add shortening and milk. Beat well. Beat in eggs and 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla. Spread batter in three greased and floured 9 inch round pans. Bake for 30 minutes at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Cool, and remove from pans. Mix 3 cups sugar, corn syrup, and 1 cup boiling water in a saucepan; boil until mixture spins thread. Beat egg whites until stiff. Pour syrup over egg whites, beating constantly. Add 1 teaspoon vanilla to flavor the icing. Frost cake, and sprinkle with coconut. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
