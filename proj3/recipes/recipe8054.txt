Recipe By:Sue Smith "This recipe is great for both yellow and chocolate flavored cakes! It makes a light, fluffy, and SERIOUSLY moist cake." Ingredients 7/8 (18.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix 1-3/4 teaspoons vanilla extract 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon sour cream 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons confectioners' sugar for dusting Directions Make the batter following the directions on the box, EXCEPT replace 2 teaspoons water with an equal amount of vanilla. Fold in sour cream. Bake according to directions given for baking a bundt cake. Cool on rack, place on serving plate, and dust with confectioners sugar.
