Recipe By:Michelle L. "Vegan bread, that's low fat and healthy. Quinoa and spelt flours are available at most health food stores." Ingredients 3/4 cup and 3 tablespoons warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C) 3/4 teaspoon sea salt 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon whole wheat flour 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon quinoa flour 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon white spelt flour 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons chopped fresh rosemary 2-1/2 teaspoons active dry yeast Directions Add ingredients in order suggested by your manufacturer. Select whole wheat setting.
