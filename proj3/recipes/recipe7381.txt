Recipe By:Joanna "A quick and easy cake--we eat it for breakfast at our house." Ingredients 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon butter 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon white sugar 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon chilled applesauce 1-2/3 cups all-purpose flour 3/4 teaspoon baking soda 3/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon raisins 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons chopped walnuts Directions Cream butter or margarine with sugar. Add applesauce; beat well. Stir in flour, soda, and spices. Add nuts and raisins. Pour the batter into a greased and floured 8 inch square pan. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 40 minutes, or until done. Serve warm.
