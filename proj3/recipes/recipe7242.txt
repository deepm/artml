Recipe By:Susan Holtgrewe "This recipe was given to me a while back and it is great on Sunday mornings. My boys just love it!" Ingredients 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon white sugar 1-2/3 cups self-rising flour 3/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons chopped walnuts 1-3/4 eggs, beaten 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon margarine, melted 7/8 apple - peeled, cored and finely chopped 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon shredded Cheddar cheese 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon milk Directions Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan. In a large bowl, combine the sugar, flour, cinnamon walnuts, apples, and cheese. Add the eggs, melted butter or margarine, and milk; stir until well blended. Pour into prepared loaf pan. Bake in preheated oven for 1 hour, or until done. If loaf starts to brown too much, cover with foil.
