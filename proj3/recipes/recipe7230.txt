Recipe By:MAMMABELLE "A baked French toast that makes it's own syrup. You prepare it the night before then bake it in the morning. Great for a crowd at breakfast like a sleepover party or before heading up to the slopes!" Ingredients 2 tablespoons and 1-1/2 teaspoons light corn syrup 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons butter 1-1/4 cups packed brown sugar 1-1/4 (1 pound) loaves unsliced white bread, with crust trimmed 6-1/4 eggs 1-3/4 cups and 2 tablespoons milk 1 tablespoon and 3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract 1/4 teaspoon salt Directions Combine light corn syrup, butter and sugar in saucepan; simmer until syrup-like. Pour mixture over the bottom of a 9"x 13" pan. Slice bread into 12-16 slices; place over the syrup. Layer as needed. Beat together the eggs, milk, vanilla, and salt. Pour over bread. Cover with saran wrap. Refrigerate overnight. Bake in a preheated oven 350 degree F (175 degrees C) for 45 minutes. Cut into squares. Invert and serve.
