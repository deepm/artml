Recipe By:Carol "This cake is very colorful, and must be eaten in the same day it is made. Good for kids. " Ingredients 2/3 cup and 2 teaspoons butter 23 marshmallows 11-1/2 cups popped popcorn 2/3 cup and 2 teaspoons small gumdrops (no black ones) 2/3 cup and 2 teaspoons chocolate covered peanuts 2/3 cup and 2 teaspoons candy-coated chocolate pieces Directions Melt butter and marshmallows in a large heavy pan over low heat. Stir often. Put popcorn into large container. Pour marshmallow mixture over top. Stir to mix well. Add peanuts and candy. Mix together. Pack mixture into greased 10 inch tube pan or 12 cup bundt pan.
