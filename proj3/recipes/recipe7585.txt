Recipe By:v monte "This simple cake recipe uses pistachio pudding mix and lemon-lime flavored carbonated beverage to deliver a cake that is great for potlucks or family gatherings." Ingredients 1/2 (18.25 ounce) package white cake mix 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon vegetable oil 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon lemon-lime flavored carbonated beverage 1-3/4 eggs 1 (3 ounce) packages instant pistachio pudding mix 1 (1.3 ounce) envelopes whipped topping mix 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon milk Directions Beat cake mix, oil, soda, eggs, and first package of pudding until smooth. Pour batter into greased 9 x 13 inch pan. Bake at 325 degrees F (165 degrees C) for 45 minutes, or cake tests done. Beat Dream Whip (TM) , milk, and pudding until stiff; pour over cooled cake.
