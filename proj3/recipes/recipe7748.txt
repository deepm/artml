Recipe By:Leo J. Lagrotte "Here is my version of chocolate cake. Aunt Mary's Vanilla Frosting compliments it perfectly." Ingredients 1-2/3 cups all-purpose flour 1-2/3 cups white sugar 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder 1-3/4 teaspoons baking soda 3/4 teaspoon baking powder 7/8 pinch salt 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon milk 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon vegetable oil 3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon hot, brewed coffee 1-3/4 eggs Directions In a large bowl, sift together flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, soda, and salt. Add oil, milk, vanilla, coffee, and eggs; beat well. Batter will be thin. Pour into two 9 inch greased and floured round pans.
