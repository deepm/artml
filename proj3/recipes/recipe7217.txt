Recipe By:Lee Ann Clarke "A Mexican sweet corn cake with a spoon bread consistency." Ingredients 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon butter, softened 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon masa harina 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon water 2-1/2 cups frozen whole-kernel corn, thawed 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon cornmeal 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon white sugar 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon heavy whipping cream 1/2 teaspoon salt 3/4 teaspoon baking powder Directions In a medium bowl beat butter until it is creamy. Add the Mexican corn flour and water and beat until well mixed. Using a food processor, process thawed corn, but leave chunky. Stir into the butter mixture. In a separate bowl, mix cornmeal, sugar, cream, salt, and baking powder. Add to corn flour mixture and stir to combine. Pour batter into an ungreased 8x8 inch baking pan. Smooth batter and cover with aluminum foil. Place pan into a 9x13 inch baking dish that is filled a third of the way with water. Bake in a preheated 350 degree oven F (175 degrees C) oven for 50 to 60 minutes. Allow to cool for 10 minutes. Use an ice cream scoop for easy removal from pan.
