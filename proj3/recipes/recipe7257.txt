Recipe By:cathie "This quick and simple recipe makes enough chocolate chip-studded cake to delight a crowd." Ingredients 2/3 cup and 1 tablespoon boiling water 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon rolled oats 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon packed brown sugar 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon white sugar 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon butter 7/8 eggs 2/3 cup and 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour 1/2 teaspoon baking soda 1-1/4 teaspoons unsweetened cocoa powder 1/4 teaspoon salt 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon semisweet chocolate chips 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon chopped walnuts Directions Mix water and oatmeal in mixing bowl, and let sit 10 minutes. Add sugars, butter or margarine, and eggs. Mix well. Add flour, baking soda, cocoa, and salt. Blend. Pour into greased and floured 10 x 15 x 1 inch jelly roll pan. Top with chocolate chips and chopped nuts. Bake in a preheated 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) oven for 20 minutes.
