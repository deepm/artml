Recipe By:Suzanne Stull "Creamy tasting syrup that's great when poured over cakes with fruit." Ingredients 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons butter 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon white sugar 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon evaporated milk 3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract Directions In a saucepan combine the butter or margarine, evaporated milk, white sugar, and the vanilla extract. Bring to a boil over medium high heat and continue to cook for 5 minutes. Pour over your choice of cake while syrup is still warm.
