Recipe By:Brenda Benzar Butler "This makes a tube pan cake that is moist and delicious. It's the rum glaze that soaks in after baking that makes it fabulous!" Ingredients 2/3 cup and 2 teaspoons butter 1-1/3 cups and 2 tablespoons white sugar 2-3/4 eggs 2/3 cup and 2 teaspoons buttermilk 3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract 2 cups and 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour 1/4 teaspoon baking powder 1/4 teaspoon baking soda 3/4 pinch salt 3/4 teaspoon rum flavored extract 2/3 cup and 2 teaspoons butter 2/3 cup and 2 teaspoons white sugar 1/3 cup and 1 teaspoon rum Directions Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour one 9 or 10 inch tube pan. Cream 1 cup of the butter and 2 cups of the white sugar together. Add eggs one at a time mixing well after each one. Sift the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt together and add alternately with the buttermilk to the egg mixture. Stir in the vanilla and rum extracts. Pour batter into prepared pan. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 1 hour. Remove cake from oven and pour all of the Rum Butter glaze over cake while still warm. Leave cake in pan for 2 hours before removing to serving dish. To Make Butter Rum Glaze: Melt the remaining 1 cup of the butter and 1 cup of white sugar over low heat. Remove from heat and stir in rum. Use immediately to glaze cake.
