Recipe By:ANGELADY41 "A light and fluffy cake with a packaged cake mix base and a wonderful, easy topping - from mixing to eating in less than 2 hours! And oh, so good!" Ingredients 1/2 (18.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix 1/2 (3.5 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix 2-1/4 eggs 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon vegetable oil 2/3 cup and 1 teaspoon lemon-lime flavored carbonated beverage 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon white sugar 1 tablespoon and 1/4 teaspoon all-purpose flour 1 eggs 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon butter 1/2 (20 ounce) can crushed pineapple with juice 1/2 (3.5 ounce) package flaked coconut Directions Grease a 9 x 13 pan, and preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Combine cake mix, pudding mix, slightly beaten eggs, and vegetable oil in a mixing bowl. Beat on medium speed until batter is light and fluffy. Add 7-UP (TM) , and beat in well. Pour batter into prepared pan. Bake for 35 to 40 minutes, or till done. While cake is baking, prepare the topping; it will take about 15 minutes to prepare. In a medium non-aluminum saucepan, combine sugar and flour. Stir in 2 eggs, butter or margarine, and pineapple. Cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until thickened.
