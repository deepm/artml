Recipe By:Carol "This cake recipe is about 60 years old. I hope that you will enjoy this one." Ingredients 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon cake flour 1/2 teaspoon salt 3/4 teaspoon baking powder 2-1/2 eggs 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon white sugar 2-1/2 teaspoons lemon juice 1/2 teaspoon lemon extract 1/4 cup and 1 tablespoon hot milk Directions Sift together flour, salt, and baking powder. In a large bowl, beat eggs until fluffy and lemon coloured. Gradually beat in sugar. Stir in lemon juice and flavoring. Add sifted flour mixture gradually while beating; beat only enough to blend. Add hot milk quickly 1 tablespoon at a time, beating until blended. Pour into ungreased pan at once. Bake 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 45 to 55 minutes, or until golden brown and firm to touch.
