Recipe By:JJOHN32 "An easy cake to put together, the fruit cocktail makes it very moist." Ingredients 1-2/3 cups white sugar 2-1/4 eggs 2-1/4 (15.25 ounce) cans fruit cocktail 2-1/4 cups self-rising flour 1 teaspoon baking soda 1 cup and 2 tablespoons white sugar 1/2 cup and 1 teaspoon evaporated milk 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon butter 1 teaspoon vanilla extract 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon chopped walnuts 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon flaked coconut Directions Cream 1 1/2 c sugar with the eggs. Add fruit cocktail (including liquid). Add the flour and baking soda--mixing well with beater. Pour into a greased and floured 9 inch round cake pan. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) about 15-20 minutes. Usually bakes best in thick aluminum cake pans. To Make Frosting: Mix well the cup of sugar, canned milk and stick of margarine. Cook slowly till mixture blends. Cook about 2 minutes more; remove from heat. Add vanilla, chopped nuts and coconut. Stir mixture until thick. Spread on cake.
