Recipe By:Bev "White cake with pistachio pudding, coconut and pecans topped with pistachio icing." Ingredients 1/2 (18.25 ounce) package white cake mix 1-3/4 eggs 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon vegetable oil 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon lemon-lime flavored carbonated beverage 1/2 (3 ounce) package instant pistachio pudding mix 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon flaked coconut 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon chopped pecans 1 (1.3 ounce) envelopes whipped topping mix 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon milk 1/2 (3 ounce) package instant pistachio pudding mix 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons chopped walnuts 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon flaked coconut Directions Mix cake mix, oil, eggs, soda drink, and 1 package pudding well, about 5 minutes. Fold in coconut and nuts. Pour into greased and floured 9 x 13 inch pan. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 45 minutes. Cool. To Make Icing: Combine dream whip, milk, and 1 package pudding. Beat until thick. Spread over cake. Sprinkle nuts and coconut on top.
