Recipe By:Kevin Ryan "This rich and creamy ricotta cheese filling is flavored with cinnamon and candied lemon peel and is a must-have when making classic Italian dessert, Cassata cake." Ingredients 1-3/4 cups and 2 tablespoons confectioners' sugar 1-3/4 pounds whole milk ricotta cheese 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon 1-1/4 teaspoons vanilla extract 1-3/4 (1 ounce) squares semisweet chocolate 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon candied lemon peel Directions Beat the ricotta cheese well and add the confectioner's sugar and the cinnamon. Add the vanilla and grate the chocolate in using the coarse side of a grater. Stir in the candied lemon peel and mix. Chill until ready to use.
