Recipe By:Glenda "Yellow cake mix, canned fruit cocktail, coconut, and nuts blend in this recipe that may look a bit 'ugly,' but tastes great." Ingredients 1/2 (18.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix 1/2 (16 ounce) can fruit cocktail 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon flaked coconut 1 eggs 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon packed brown sugar 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon chopped walnuts 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon butter 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon white sugar 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon evaporated milk Directions Combine cake mix, undrained fruit, 1 cup coconut and eggs. Blend; beat at medium speed for 2 minutes and pour into a greased 9 x 13 inch pan. Sprinkle with brown sugar and nuts. Bake at 325 degrees F (165 degrees C) for 45 minutes. Bring butter, sugar and milk to a boil. Boil 1 minute. Stir in 1/2 cup coconut. Spoon over warm cake. Top with prepared whipped topping, if desired.
