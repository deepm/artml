Recipe By:Stephanie "This is for the Cheesecake/Rocky Road lovers! I hope you like it as much as I do! Garnish with miniature marshmallows and chocolate chips if desired." Ingredients 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon chocolate cookie crumbs 2 tablespoons and 1-1/2 teaspoons unsalted butter 7/8 (.25 ounce) package unflavored gelatin 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon cold water 1-3/4 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons white sugar 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon unsweetened cocoa powder 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract 1-2/3 cups miniature marshmallows 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons chopped walnuts 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon heavy cream Directions Melt the butter or margarine, and combine with the cookie crumbs. Press mixture into bottom of 9 inch springform pan. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 10 minutes. In a small saucepan, soften gelatin in water; stir over low heat until dissolved. In a large bowl, combine cream cheese, sugar, cocoa, and vanilla; blend with an electric mixer on medium speed. Gradually add gelatin mixture, and blend.
