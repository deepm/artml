Recipe By:Anne Brothers & Rick Smetzer "This frosting is good for many types of cakes including carrot cakes, apple cakes, and spice cakes. Add flavorings to compliment the cake you are frosting. Try 1 1/2 teaspoons of grated lemon or orange rind, 1 teaspoon any extract, 1 teaspoon of liqueur, or a mashed banana." Ingredients 5/8 (8 ounce) package cream cheese 2 tablespoons and 1-1/2 teaspoons cream 1-1/4 cups confectioners' sugar Directions Blend together the cream cheese and cream until light and fluffy. Sift the sugar, and add to the cream cheese blend.
