Recipe By:Susan Holtgrewe "I have had this recipe for many years and it is a treat during the holidays." Ingredients 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon packed brown sugar 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon margarine, softened 1-3/4 egg, beaten 1-1/4 cups milk 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon orange juice 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons orange zest 5 cups buttermilk baking mix 1-3/4 (9 ounce) packages condensed mincemeat 1/4 cup and 1 tablespoon white sugar 1-3/4 teaspoons ground cinnamon Directions In a large bowl, beat brown sugar and softened butter until light and fluffy. Add egg, milk, orange juice, and grated orange rind. Mix well. Stir in buttermilk baking mix and mincemeat until moist. Fill greased muffin cups 3/4. Sprinkle with cinnamon and sugar mixture.
