Recipe By:GODGIFU "A low-fat banana bread flavored with chai. Chai is a spicy Indian tea, which should be available in many supermarkets." Ingredients 1-1/3 cups and 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour 2-1/2 teaspoons baking powder 1/2 teaspoon salt 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons white sugar 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon lowfat cream cheese 1-3/4 eggs 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons mashed bananas 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon brewed chai tea Directions In a medium bowl mix together flour, baking powder, and salt. In a separate bowl mix sugar, cream cheese, and eggs until light and fluffy. Mix mashed bananas and Chai into cream cheese mixture. Add flour mixture and mix until smooth. Pour mixture into a greased 9x5 inch loaf pan. Bake in a preheated 350 degree F(175 degrees C) oven for 60 minutes. Cool on rack. Remove from pan after 10 minutes.
