Recipe By:roger russell "A strong tasting cheese bread." Ingredients 1 cup and 1 tablespoon warm water 2-1/2 cups bread flour 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon shredded pepperjack cheese 1-3/4 teaspoons Italian seasoning 3/4 teaspoon ground black pepper 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons grated Parmesan cheese 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons brown sugar 1-1/4 teaspoons salt 1-3/4 teaspoons active dry yeast Directions Place ingredients in the pan of the bread machine in the order suggested by the manufacturer.
