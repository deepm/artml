Recipe By:Harriet "Easy to make cake. Dust with confectioners' sugar, no frosting needed." Ingredients 1/2 (18.25 ounce) package white cake mix 3 tablespoons vegetable oil 2-1/4 eggs 1/4 pound sour cream 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon unsweetened cocoa powder 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon semisweet chocolate chips 1/2 banana, peeled and mashed Directions Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease and flour one 9x13 inch pan. Mix together cake mix, oil, eggs, sour cream, cocoa, chocolate chips, and banana, and beat for two minutes. Pour into prepared pan.
