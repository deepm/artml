Recipe By:Carol "This gingerbread differs from our own North American one although both have European origins." Ingredients 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon butter, softened 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon white sugar 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon molasses 1 eggs 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon beer 3/4 cup and 3 tablespoons and 2 teaspoons all-purpose flour 3/4 cup and 3 tablespoons and 2 teaspoons whole wheat flour 1-3/4 teaspoons ground ginger 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon 1/8 teaspoon ground cloves 1/8 teaspoon ground nutmeg 1/2 teaspoon baking soda 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon raisins 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon diced candied orange peel 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon finely chopped almonds Directions Stir together the flours, spices, and baking soda. In a large bowl, cream the butter or margarine with the sugar and molasses. Beat in the eggs, then the beer. Beat the flour mixture into the creamed mixture. Stir in the raisins, orange peel, and almonds. Turn the batter into a greased and floured 9 x 13 inch baking pan. Bake at 325 degrees F (165 degrees C) for 40 minutes, or until done. Transfer to a rack to cool.
