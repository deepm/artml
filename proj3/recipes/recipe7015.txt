Recipe By:Mitzi Lyons "This cornbread is considered a favorite in our family. It was given to me by my mother-in-law. It is a very thick and hearty cornbread and goes well with most anything. You can also add tomatoes and ground beef." Ingredients 1-1/4 cups cornmeal 1-1/4 cups milk 3-3/4 eggs 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons chopped green bell pepper 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons chopped onion 1-1/4 teaspoons garlic powder 1/2 teaspoon salt 1/2 teaspoon baking soda 1/2 teaspoon white sugar 1-1/4 (8.75 ounce) cans whole kernel corn, drained 1-1/4 (4 ounce) jars diced pimento peppers, drained 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons vegetable oil 2-1/2 teaspoons chopped jalapeno peppers Directions Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Combine the cornmeal, milk, oil, eggs, chopped green bell pepper, chopped onion, garlic powder, salt, baking soda, sugar, drained corn kernels, pimentos and jalapeno peppers. Mix until combined, and pour into a well greased 10 to 14 inch cast iron skillet. Bake for 45 minutes.
