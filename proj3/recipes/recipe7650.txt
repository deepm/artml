Recipe By:Kevin Ryan "This chocolate buttercream recipe delivers creamy, chocolate-y goodness for slathering on your favorite cakes, cookies, or spoons." Ingredients 6 (1 ounce) squares bittersweet chocolate 1 cup unsalted butter 1/2 pinch salt 1 eggs 2 cups confectioners' sugar 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract Directions Melt the bittersweet chocolate, and allow to cool slightly. Place the butter or margarine, salt, and vanilla in a mixing bowl. Beat with a mixer until very light and airy, about 4 minutes. Add the powdered sugar a little at a time while beating on low speed. Mix well, and beat on medium speed for about 4 minutes. Add the eggs one at a time, and beat for 5 minutes more. Add the melted chocolate, and beat 4 minutes.
