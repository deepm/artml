Recipe By:Carol "Frosting for General Robert E. Lee Cake or use your own white or lemon cake recipes. Enough to cover the sides and top of a 4 - 9 inch layer cake." Ingredients 3 cups confectioners' sugar 1/2 egg yolk 1 tablespoon lemon juice 1 tablespoon and 1-1/2 teaspoons orange juice 1/2 lemon 1 large oranges 2 tablespoons butter Directions Beat the butter until it has the appearance of thick cream, stir in confectioners sugar, a little at a time, and continue working until mixture is very smooth. Beat in egg yolk and lemon juice. Stir in enough orange juice to make a spreadable frosting, then add grated rind of 1 lemon and 2 grated orange rinds. Spread on sides and top of cake.
