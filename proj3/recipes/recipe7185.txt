Recipe By:Janet "Use as you would a baking mix. The possibilities are endless!" Ingredients 4 cups and 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon baking powder 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons white sugar 3/4 teaspoon salt 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon shortening Directions In a large bowl mix together the flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt. Cut in shortening until mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Store in an airtight container for up to 6 weeks. ALL RIGHTS RESERVED © 2019 Allrecipes.com Printed From Allrecipes.com 3/29/2019
