Recipe By:Rhonda "This recipe makes a red cake frosted with cream cheese frosting to stand as a bright spot on your holiday table." Ingredients 2 cups and 1 tablespoon self-rising flour 1-1/4 cups white sugar 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon vegetable oil 3/4 teaspoon baking soda 3/4 teaspoon distilled white vinegar 3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract 1-3/4 eggs 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon buttermilk 1-1/2 ounces red food coloring 7/8 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon butter, softened 3-1/3 cups confectioners' sugar 3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon chopped pecans Directions Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour three 8 inch round pans. In a large bowl, mix together sugar, oil, and eggs. Add food coloring and vinegar to buttermilk. Add baking soda to flour. Add flour mixture and buttermilk mixtures alternately to the sugar mixture. Mix well. Stir 1 teaspoon vanilla into batter. Pour batter into prepared pans. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes, or until done. Remove from oven, and cool on wire racks. Mix together cream cheese, butter or margarine, confectioners' sugar, 1 teaspoon vanilla. Stir in nuts. Frost cooled cake.
