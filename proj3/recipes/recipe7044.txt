Recipe By:Suzy "Easy recipe for challah or egg bread using a bread machine. I use the light setting on my bread machine." Ingredients 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons milk 1-3/4 eggs 2 tablespoons and 1-1/2 teaspoons margarine 2-1/2 cups bread flour 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon white sugar 1-1/4 teaspoons salt 1-1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast Directions Add ingredients to the pan of the bread machine in the order suggested by the manufacturer.
