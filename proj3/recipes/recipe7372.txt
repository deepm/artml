Recipe By:Mona "Tangy lemonade flavored whipped cream frosting works very well with yellow or white cakes." Ingredients 1-1/4 cups heavy whipping cream 1 tablespoon and 3/4 teaspoon white sugar 3 tablespoons and 2-1/4 teaspoons frozen pink lemonade concentrate, thawed Directions In a large glass, metal , or ceramic bowl beat whipping cream, sugar, and pink lemonade until fluffy and peaks form. Use immediately.
