Recipe By:Carol "This is an old recipe I have for Mocha Icing. I hope you like it. The recipe makes enough for one 8 inch layer cake." Ingredients 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon unsweetened cocoa powder 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon strong, hot, brewed coffee 2-1/2 cups confectioners' sugar 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon melted butter 1/8 teaspoon salt 3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract 1/4 cup and 1 tablespoon chopped walnuts (optional) Directions Combine coffee and cocoa in small bowl of electric mixer. Beat until blended. Add sugar, butter or margarine, salt, and vanilla. Beat until blended. Scrape bowl, and beat icing until it has a spreading consistency. Frost the cake, and sprinkle with chopped walnuts if desired.
