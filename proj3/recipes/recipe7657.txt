Recipe By:Carol "This recipe is over 100 years old and it tastes like hermit cookies. Use only butter in this recipe." Ingredients 1-1/3 cups and 2 tablespoons butter, softened 2 cups and 2 tablespoons packed brown sugar 4-1/4 eggs 3/4 lemon, juiced 2-3/4 teaspoons vanilla extract 3-1/4 cups all-purpose flour 2-3/4 teaspoons baking powder 1-1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon 1/8 teaspoon salt 2 cups and 2 tablespoons dates, pitted and chopped 3 cups and 2 tablespoons chopped walnuts Directions Preheat oven to 275 degrees F (135 degrees C). Cream butter and sugar together well. Beat in eggs one at a time. Mix in lemon juice and vanilla. Add remaining ingredients. Stir well. Turn into greased and floured 10 inch angel food tube pan.
