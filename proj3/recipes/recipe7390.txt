Recipe By:Lil "I make it with a Butterscotch Filling and a Chocolate Glaze." Ingredients 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon butter 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons packed brown sugar 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons white sugar 2-1/2 eggs 1-1/4 cups all-purpose flour 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons whole wheat flour 2 teaspoons baking powder 3/4 teaspoon salt 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon milk 1-1/4 teaspoons vanilla extract 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon semisweet chocolate chips Directions Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour two 9 inch round pans. In a large mixing bowl, cream together butter and sugars. Beat in the eggs. In another bowl, sift together the flours, baking powder, and salt. Add this alternately with the milk to the creamed mixture. Stir in the vanilla. Chop the chocolate chips coarsely, and add to the batter. Pour batter into prepared pans, and bake for 25 to 30 minutes, or until knife inserted in middle comes out clean. You can fill with a Butterscotch Filling, and top with a Chocolate Glaze.
