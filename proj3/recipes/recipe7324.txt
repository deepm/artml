Recipe By:Erica "This is an old fashioned sponge cake with a lemon flavor." Ingredients 4-1/4 eggs 2/3 cup and 2 teaspoons white sugar 2 tablespoons and 2-1/2 teaspoons water 3/4 teaspoon lemon extract 3/4 teaspoon lemon zest 2/3 cup and 2 teaspoons cake flour 1/4 teaspoon cream of tartar 1/8 teaspoon salt Directions Separate the eggs. In a large mixing bowl, beat egg yolks until very thick and lemon colored. Beat in sugar gradually. Add water, lemon extract and lemon rind. Beat in flour. In another bowl, beat egg whites until frothy. Then add cream of tartar and salt. Beat mixture until whites are stiff, but not until they are dry. Fold this whipped mixture into yolk mixture.
