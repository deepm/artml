Recipe By:Sally E. Forsythe "If you have a sweet tooth or are a chocoholic, this is the cake for you! Make this cake, and it will not stay long in your house!" Ingredients 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon vegetable oil 1-1/4 (1 ounce) squares semi-sweet chocolate 5/8 egg 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons white sugar 1/3 cup and 2 tablespoons milk 3/4 cup and 2 teaspoons all-purpose flour 1/4 teaspoon salt 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract 1/4 teaspoon baking soda 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons semisweet chocolate chips 1/3 cup and 2 tablespoons chopped walnuts Directions Combine oil, unsweetened chocolate, egg, sugar, milk, flour, salt, vanilla, and baking soda in a 9 inch square pan. Mix until smooth. Sprinkle with nuts and chocolate chips. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 30 minutes. Cool on wire racks.
