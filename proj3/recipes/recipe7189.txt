Recipe By:Amy Barry "This is a quick bread that my mom used to make every summer with the zucchinis from the garden." Ingredients 1-1/4 eggs, beaten 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon white sugar 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon vegetable oil 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon grated zucchini 1-1/4 cups all-purpose flour 1/2 teaspoon baking soda 1/2 teaspoon baking powder 1/2 teaspoon salt 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon raisins 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon chopped pecans 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon crushed pineapple, drained Directions Mix together the eggs, sugar, vanilla, oil, and zucchini in a large bowl. In a separate bowl mix together the flour, soda, baking powder, and salt. Add to the zucchini batter and mix well. Stir in raisins, nuts, and pineapple until just blended. Pour into 2-9x5x3 inch loaf pans. Bake in a preheated 325 degree F (165 degrees C) for one hour, or until browned. ALL RIGHTS RESERVED © 2019 Allrecipes.com Printed From Allrecipes.com 3/29/2019
