Recipe By:Carol "Make your own marzipan instead of buying it. Marzipan is used to frost Christmas cakes and for candies, and all sorts of other goodies." Ingredients 1-1/4 cups confectioners' sugar 5 ounces finely ground blanched almonds 1-1/4 egg whites 1/4 teaspoon salt 1/4 teaspoon almond extract Directions Blend the confectioner's sugar, finely ground almonds, egg whites, salt ,and almond extract in a blender until perfectly blended. Chill covered 24 hours to harden.
