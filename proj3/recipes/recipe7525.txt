Recipe By:JJOHN32 "This Bundt(R) cake made with 7-UP(R) is features only a few ingredients and steps for an easy dessert." Ingredients 1-1/4 cups butter 2-1/2 cups white sugar 4-1/4 eggs 2-1/2 cups all-purpose flour 1-3/4 teaspoons lemon extract 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons lemon-lime flavored carbonated beverage Directions Cream together the butter and sugar for 20 minutes. Add eggs, one at a time. Add flour, lemon extract and fold in the 7UP (TM) soft drink. Pour into a well-greased 12 cup Bundt pan. Bake at 325 degrees F (165 degrees C) for 60 to 75 minutes.
