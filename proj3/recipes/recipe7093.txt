Recipe By:Danielle "A white bread recipe without a strong yeast flavor. Proofing the yeast first eliminates some of the yeast taste from the final product." Ingredients 1-3/4 teaspoons active dry yeast 2 tablespoons and 2 teaspoons warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C) 2 teaspoons white sugar 2-2/3 cups all-purpose flour 2 tablespoons and 2 teaspoons dry potato flakes 2 tablespoons and 2 teaspoons dry milk powder 1-1/4 teaspoons salt 2 tablespoons and 2 teaspoons white sugar 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon margarine 2/3 cup warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C) Directions Whisk together the yeast, 1/4 cup warm water and sugar. Allow to sit for 15 minutes. Add ingredients in the order suggested by your manufacturer, including the yeast mixture. Select the basic and light crust setting.
