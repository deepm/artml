Recipe By:Kevin Ryan "This is a very easy frosting to make, not too sweet, and absolutely outstanding" Ingredients 1-3/4 (1 ounce) squares unsweetened chocolate, melted and cooled 7/8 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon water 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract 7/8 (3.9 ounce) package instant chocolate pudding mix 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon heavy cream, whipped Directions Blend the melted chocolate and milk. Gradually mix in the water, vanilla, and pudding mix, and whisk until smooth. Fold in the heavy cream. Frost the cake immediately, and chill at least 30 minutes before serving.
