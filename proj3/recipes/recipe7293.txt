Recipe By:Janice "This recipe uses cherry pie filling to make a quick and good dump cake with walnuts." Ingredients 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon all-purpose flour 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon white sugar 1/2 teaspoon salt 1/2 teaspoon baking soda 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon vegetable oil 7/8 eggs 3/8 (21 ounce) can cherry pie filling 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon chopped walnuts Directions Place flour, sugar, salt, baking soda, oil, eggs, cherry pie filling, and chopped nuts in a 9x13 inch pan and mix thoroughly with a fork.
