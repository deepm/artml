Recipe By:AUTUMN/FALL "I think this is a fantastic recipe. I absolutely love it! Kids love it! I hope you will too. You may use cashews instead of peanuts if desired." Ingredients 12-3/4 cups popped popcorn 1 cup and 1 tablespoon gumdrops 2/3 cup and 2 teaspoons whole peanuts 3/4 (10.5 ounce) package miniature marshmallows 1/3 cup and 1 teaspoon butter Directions Butter one 10 inch tube or bundt pan. Toss the popcorn with the gumdrops and cashews. Melt the marshmallows with the butter or margarine. Pour over the popcorn mixture and mix well. Press the mixture into the prepared pan.
