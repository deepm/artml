Recipe By:Meredith "An EASY, moist, and delicious cake. You just dump all the ingredients in a bowl and mix. PERFECT for 1st-time bakers!" Ingredients 7/8 (18.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix 7/8 (5.9 ounce) package instant chocolate pudding mix 3-1/4 eggs, beaten 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon vegetable oil 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon white sugar 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon water 7/8 (8 ounce) container sour cream 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon semisweet chocolate chips Directions Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour a Bundt pan. In a bowl, mix the yellow cake mix, pudding mix, eggs, vegetable oil, sugar, and water. Gently fold in the sour cream and chocolate chips. Pour batter into the prepared Bundt pan. Bake in the preheated oven for 55 minutes. Cool in pan for 10 minutes before transferring to cooling racks.
