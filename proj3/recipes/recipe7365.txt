Recipe By:Mariann "This is an easy recipe for a rum-soaked cake filled with walnuts and a rum glaze." Ingredients 1 cup and 3 tablespoons chopped walnuts 7/8 (18.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon dark rum 3-1/4 eggs 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon water 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon vegetable oil 7/8 (3.5 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix Glaze 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon butter 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons water 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon white sugar 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon rum Directions Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Grease and flour a 10 inch tube or Bundt pan. Sprinkle nuts over the bottom of the pan. Mix together the cake mix, 1/2 cup dark rum, eggs, 1/2 cup water, oil, and vanilla pudding mix. Pour batter over the nuts in the pan. Bake for 1 hour. Cool, and invert cake on a serving plate. Prick the top of the cake. To Make The Glaze: Melt the butter in a saucepan. Stir in 1/8 cup water and the 1/2 cup sugar. Boil for 5 minutes, stirring constantly. Remove glaze from heat, and stir in 1/4 cup rum. Drizzle and smooth evenly over the top and sides.
