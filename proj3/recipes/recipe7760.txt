Recipe By:Geri Knoll "This is a moist spice cake with a surprise ingredient!" Ingredients 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon white sugar 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon shortening 1/4 teaspoon salt 1 eggs 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg 1/2 teaspoon baking powder 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon raisins 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon chopped walnuts 1 cup and 2 tablespoons sifted all-purpose flour 1/2 (10.75 ounce) can condensed tomato soup 1/2 teaspoon baking soda 1/2 (8 ounce) package cream cheese 1 cup and 2 tablespoons confectioners' sugar Directions Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease and flour a 9 x 13 inch pan. In a large bowl, cream sugar and shortening. Add beaten eggs, and mix well. Stir in salt, cinnamon, nutmeg, and cloves. Mix in flour and baking powder, and then raisins and nuts. Mix tomato soup and baking soda together; beat into first mixture. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes. Cool.
