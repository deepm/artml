Recipe By:Carol "This easy cake recipe makes a good lemon-flavored cake perfect for any occasion." Ingredients 2 cups and 1 tablespoon sifted cake flour 2 teaspoons baking powder 1/2 teaspoon salt 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon butter 2-1/2 teaspoons lemon zest 1-1/3 cups and 2 tablespoons white sugar 5 egg yolks 3/4 cup and 3 tablespoons milk Directions Line two 9 inch round pans with parchment paper. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Measure sifted flour, baking powder, and salt; sift together three times. Cream butter or margarine and lemon rind together. Gradually add sugar, creaming until light and fluffy. Add egg yolks one at a time, beating thoroughly after each addition. Add sifted ingredients alternately with milk to the creamed mixture, a small amount at a time; beat after each addition until smooth. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes. Cool on wire racks.
