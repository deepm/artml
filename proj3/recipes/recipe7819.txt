Recipe By:Carol "Lemony whipped cream filling. A wonderful filling for white, yellow, or citrus cakes." Ingredients 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons white sugar 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons all-purpose flour 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons grated orange zest 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon orange juice 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons fresh lemon juice 7/8 egg 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon whipped cream Directions In a saucepan over medium heat mix the white sugar, flour, orange juice, orange zest, lemon juice and the egg. Cook until thickened. Remove from heat and let cool completely before folding in the whipped cream. Makes enough to fill one 2 layer 9 inch round layer cake.
