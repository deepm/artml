Recipe By:Liz B. "Oatmeal Molasses Bread via the bread machine. I often just use the dough cycle, take the dough out when done, and let it rise on more time. I then bake it in the oven at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for about 28 minutes. The texture is just like a real homemade loaf. Enjoy!!!!!" Ingredients 7/8 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon quick cooking oats 2-1/2 cups bread flour 3/4 teaspoon salt 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon molasses 2-1/2 teaspoons vegetable oil 1 cup and 2 tablespoons water 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon honey Directions Place ingredients in the pan of the bread machine in the order suggested by the manufacturer.
