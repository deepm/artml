Recipe By:Carol "This is quite an old recipe, and I hope that you will enjoy this one." Ingredients 3-1/2 egg whites 2 tablespoons and 2-1/2 teaspoons white sugar 1/4 teaspoon salt 3-1/2 egg yolks 2 tablespoons and 2-1/2 teaspoons white sugar 1/3 cup and 1 teaspoon molasses 3/4 teaspoon lemon zest 1-1/2 teaspoons lemon juice 1/2 cup and 2 teaspoons cake flour Directions Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Beat egg whites to form moist peaks. Gradually beat in 1/4 cup white sugar and salt. Beat until very stiff and shiny. Beat egg yolks and 1/4 cup white sugar together until very light and fluffy. Beat in molasses, grated lemon rind and lemon juice. Stir in cake flour. Fold meringue gently into batter. Pour batter into an ungreased 9 inch tube pan. Bake for 45 minutes.
