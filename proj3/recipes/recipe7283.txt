Recipe By:Delores "This easy recipe makes a wonderfully moist pound cake with plenty of butter and pecans." Ingredients 1 cup and 1 tablespoon butter 1-1/3 cups and 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour 3-1/2 eggs 1-1/3 cups and 2 tablespoons white sugar 3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract 3/4 teaspoon butter flavored extract 2/3 cup and 2 teaspoons chopped pecans 2 tablespoons and 2-1/2 teaspoons confectioners' sugar for dusting Directions Cream together butter and sugar. Add eggs one at a time beating after each one. Blend in the flour, vanilla extract, butter extract and pecans. Pour into a greased and floured bundt or tube pan. Bake in a preheated 325 degrees F (165 degrees C) oven for 1 to 1 1/2 hours or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
