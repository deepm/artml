Recipe By:PERRAULT46 "Orange cake with pineapple frosting - perfect for spring!" Ingredients 1/4 (18.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix 1 eggs 1/2 (3.5 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix 2 tablespoons and 3/4 teaspoon vegetable oil 1/2 teaspoon orange extract 1/4 (11 ounce) can mandarin orange segments 1/4 (12 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed 1/4 (8 ounce) can crushed pineapple, drained Directions Combine cake mix, eggs, 1 package of pudding, vegetable oil, orange extract, and mandarin oranges and beat well for about 3 minutes. Bake in 3 - 9 inch greased and floured round cake pans for 20-25 minutes in a pre-heated 350 degree F (175 degrees C) oven.
