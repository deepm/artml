Recipe By:Krissyp "Great during the summer. Can assemble with favorite toppings and cook right on the grill! You may use whole wheat flour in this recipe if you prefer." Ingredients 1-2/3 cups all-purpose flour 1/2 teaspoon salt 1/2 teaspoon white sugar 7/8 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast 2-1/2 teaspoons olive oil 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons cornmeal for dusting 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C) Directions Proof yeast in warm water. In separate bowl place flour, salt, sugar, and oil. Make a well in the center and place yeast/warm water mixture in. Mix thoroughly until consistency of an elastic ball. Allow to rise 1 1/2hours. Flour surface and roll out 1/2 dough. Rub with cornmeal. Sprinkle with your favorite toppings. Spray grill with cooking spray. Grill dough about 5 minutes or until toppings are melted. Repeat with second piece of dough.
