Recipe By:Carol "Great flavor and so creamy. Great on Daffodil cake." Ingredients 5/8 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened 2 tablespoons and 2 teaspoons butter 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon lemon juice 1-1/4 teaspoons lemon zest 3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract 3-1/3 cups confectioners' sugar Directions Beat cream cheese, butter, lemon juice, lemon rind, and vanilla together until smooth and fluffy. Add confectioners' sugar in 2 additions. Beat until creamy. Add more icing sugar or juice as needed for easy spreading. Makes about 3 2/3 cups.
