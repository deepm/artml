Recipe By:Carol "This is a lovely cake for special occasions. A lovely blend." Ingredients 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon butter 1-2/3 cups white sugar 2-1/2 eggs, separated 3-1/3 cups sifted all-purpose flour 1 tablespoon and 1/4 teaspoon baking powder 1 cup and 2 tablespoons milk 1-3/4 teaspoons vanilla extract Directions Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Cream 1/2 cup butter and 1 cup sugar; add egg yolks, mixing well. Combine 2 cups flour and 2 teaspoons baking powder. Add flour mixture alternately with 2/3 cup milk to creamed mixture. Pour into two greased and floured 9 inch round pans. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes, until done. Cool. Cream 1 cup sugar and 1/2 cup butter. Combine 2 cups flour and 2 teaspoons baking powder; add to creamed mixture alternately with 2/3 cup milk. Beat egg whites, and fold into batter. Stir in flavoring. Pour into two greased and floured 9 inch round pans. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes. Cool. Spread your choice of frosting between layers, alternating yellow and white layers. Frost outside of cake.
