Recipe By:APARSONS "White wedding cake frosting. This is a very good frosting for decorated cakes." Ingredients 1/2 cup shortening 2 tablespoons and 2 teaspoons water 1/8 teaspoon salt 3/4 teaspoon clear imitation vanilla extract 1/8 teaspoon powdered butter flavoring 2-2/3 cups confectioners' sugar Directions With an electric mixer blend all ingredients until mixed.
