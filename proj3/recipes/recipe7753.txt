Recipe By:Carol "This recipe gives you a cake filled with nuts and maraschino cherries for a nice, special-occasion cake." Ingredients 1-3/4 cups and 2 tablespoons sifted cake flour 2-3/4 teaspoons baking powder 3/4 teaspoon salt 1-1/4 cups white sugar 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon shortening 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon milk 1-1/4 teaspoons vanilla extract 1/4 teaspoon almond extract 3-1/4 egg whites 3/4 cup and 2 tablespoons and 2 teaspoons chopped walnuts 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons maraschino cherries, chopped 1 recipe Seven Minute Frosting 6-3/4 maraschino cherries, halved Directions Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Line the bottoms of two 9 inch round pans, or one 9 x 13 inch rectangular pan, with parchment paper. In a mixing bowl, stir shortening to soften. Sift in flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar. Add 3/4 cup of the milk and the flavorings, and mix until all flour is dampened. Beat well, with an electric mixer if possible. Add unbeaten egg whites and remaining milk, and beat well. Stir in nuts and cherries. Pour batter into prepared pans. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes. Cool completely.
