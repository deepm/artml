Recipe By:Maggie "This recipe came from my 90-year-old grandmother's handwritten recipe collection." Ingredients 2/3 cup buttermilk 3/4 teaspoon baking soda 2/3 cup white sugar 1-1/3 cups all-purpose flour 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon 2 tablespoons and 2 teaspoons butter 1/3 cup fresh blackberries 1/3 cup raisins Directions Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour three 8 inch round cake layer pans. Dissolve the baking soda in the buttermilk. Combine the sugar, 3 1/2 cups of the flour, ground nutmeg, ground cloves, ground cinnamon and the butter. Stir in the buttermilk mixture. Toss the blackberries and raisins with the remaining 1/2 cup of flour to coat. Mix in the blackberries and the raisins into the batter and stir until evenly distributed. Pour batter into the prepared pans. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 25 to 35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted near the center of the cakes comes out clean.
