Recipe By:Gudny Bjorg Kjaerbo "Bake a bliss for your love." Ingredients 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon butter 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons packed brown sugar 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons rolled oats 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons flaked coconut 5/8 egg 1/2 teaspoon baking soda 1/4 cup and 1 tablespoon rhubarb jam Directions Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease one 10-inch round cake pan. Place butter, brown sugar, flour, oatmeal, coconut, egg and baking soda in a bowl and mix well. Put 2/3 of the batter in the prepared pan then spread the rhubarb jam over it. Pat the remaining dough over the top. Bake in preheated oven until a tester inserted in the center comes out clean, 35 to 45 minutes.
