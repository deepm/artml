Recipe By:V. Monte "Really good and sweet dessert I got from a friend." Ingredients 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon butter 3/8 (18.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix 1-1/4 eggs 3/8 (8 ounce) package cream cheese 1/4 teaspoon almond extract 1-2/3 cups confectioners' sugar Directions Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease one 9x13 inch cake pan. Melt the butter or margarine. Stir melted butter or margarine along with 1 egg into the cake mix. Press into prepared pan. In a large bowl mix cream cheese, almond extract, confectioner's sugar, and the remaining 2 eggs beat for 3 minutes with an electric mixer set on medium high speed. Spread over top of cake mixture.
