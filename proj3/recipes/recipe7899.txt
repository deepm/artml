Recipe By:Carol "This cake is nice and moist. Great for gift giving. Walnuts and pecans are interchangeable in this recipe." Ingredients 1/2 cup and 2 teaspoons butter, softened 1 cup and 1 tablespoon white sugar 2-3/4 eggs 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon applesauce 1/2 cup and 2 teaspoons milk 1 cup and 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour 1 cup and 1 tablespoon rolled oats 1/8 teaspoon salt 2-1/4 teaspoons baking powder 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves 1 cup and 1 tablespoon raisins 1-1/2 cups and 1 tablespoon coarsely chopped walnuts Directions Stir together flour, oats, salt, baking powder, and spices. In a large bowl, cream the butter or margarine with the sugar. Beat in the eggs, then the applesauce and milk. Beat flour mixture into applesauce mixture. Stir in the raisins and nuts. Turn the batter into a greased and floured tube pan. Bake the cake in a preheated oven at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 65 minutes, or until it tests done. Let cake cool on a wire rack.
