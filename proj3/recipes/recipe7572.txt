Recipe By:Carol "For those of you looking for a good groom's cake, here is my recipe." Ingredients 1-3/4 cups and 1 tablespoon raisins, finely chopped 1 cup and 3 tablespoons golden raisins 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons dried currants 5 ounces chopped candied citron 1-1/2 ounces candied lemon peel 1-1/2 ounces chopped candied orange peel 2/3 cup all-purpose flour 3/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon 3/4 teaspoon ground allspice 1/4 teaspoon ground mace 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon butter 1/3 cup and 2 tablespoons packed brown sugar 2-1/2 eggs, separated 3 tablespoons and 1/2 teaspoon brandy 3/4 (1 ounce) square semisweet chocolate 3 tablespoons and 1/2 teaspoon molasses 1/8 teaspoon baking soda 3 tablespoons and 1/2 teaspoon hot water Directions In a large bowl, sift 3 cups flour with spices. In another bowl, mix raisins, sultana raisins, currants, citron, and citrus rinds; dredge with 1/3 cup flour. Stir raisin mixture into the flour. In a very large bowl, cream the butter or margarine. Beat in sugar, a small amount at a time. Add beaten egg yolks, flour mixture, brandy, chocolate and molasses. Beat egg whites until stiff, and fold them into the batter. Dissolve baking soda in hot water and stir gently into batter. Grease and flour 5 bread pans, and pour batter into prepared pans. Bake at 275 degrees F (135 degrees C) for 4 hours. Cool. Cut into 275 to 300 slices; wrap each slice.
