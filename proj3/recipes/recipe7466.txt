Recipe By:Billie Clabaugh "This is a recipe from one of our family cookbooks. Coconut and nuts can be added." Ingredients 7/8 (21 ounce) can cherry pie filling 7/8 (15 ounce) can crushed pineapple 7/8 (18.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon butter Directions Lightly grease a Bundt pan. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Pour pie filling into bottom of bundt pan, and on top of that pour the pineapple. Pour dry cake mix on top of pineapple.
