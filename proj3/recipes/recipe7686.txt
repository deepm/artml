Recipe By:JJOHN32 "Children will love the cake you make from this recipe for a simple chocolate cake with a simple chocolate frosting." Ingredients 1-1/4 cups all-purpose flour 1-2/3 cups white sugar 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon unsweetened cocoa powder 3/4 teaspoon baking soda 3/4 teaspoon salt 2-1/2 teaspoons distilled white vinegar 1-3/4 teaspoons vanilla extract 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon vegetable oil 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon water 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon butter 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon milk Directions Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease one 9 inch square pan. Sift together the flour, 1 cup of the sugar, 1/4 cup of the cocoa, the soda, and the salt. Stir in the vinegar, oil, water, and 1 teaspoon vanilla. Beat until smooth. Pour batter into the prepared pan. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 30 to 35 minutes. Let cake cool. Then spread chocolate icing over top. To Make Chocolate Icing: Combine 1 cup of the sugar, 1/4 cup of the cocoa, the milk, and the butter or margarine. Boil for 1 minute. Stir in 1 teaspoon vanilla and continue stirring until partially cool. Spread over cake.
