Recipe By:Stephanie "War Cake uses ingredients that were available to the average household during World War II. Great served with whipped topping!" Ingredients 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon packed brown sugar 2/3 cup and 1 teaspoon water 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon raisins 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon chopped walnuts 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon chopped candied citron 3 tablespoons shortening 1/4 teaspoon salt 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon 1 cup and 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour 2-3/4 teaspoons baking powder Directions Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking pan. In a large saucepan over medium heat, combine brown sugar and water. Add raisins, nuts, candied citron, shortening, salt, nutmeg, and cinnamon; bring to a boil and boil 3 minutes. Remove from heat and cool. Sift in flour and baking powder; stir until well blended. Pour into prepared pan. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 45 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. Remove from oven and cool on a wire rack.
