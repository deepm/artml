Recipe By:MARBALET "You can vary the flavor of this bread with your choice of olive - Kalamata, Amfisa, Arbequina, Niscoise ... ... ...." Ingredients 2 cups bread flour 1-1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon white sugar 3/4 teaspoon salt 1/3 cup chopped black olives 2 tablespoons olive oil 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C) 2 teaspoons cornmeal Directions In a large bowl, mix together flour, yeast, sugar, salt, black olives, olive oil, and water. Turn out dough onto a floured board. Knead until smooth and elastic, 5 to 10 minutes. Set aside, and let rise about 45 minutes, until it doubles in size. Punch down. Knead well again, for about 5 to 10 minutes. Let rise for about 30 minutes, until it doubles in size. Round the dough on kneading board. Place upside down in a bowl lined with a lint-free, well floured towel. Let rise until double in size. While the bread is rising for the third time, put a pan of water in the bottom of the oven. Preheat oven to 500 degrees F (260 degrees C). Gently turn loaf out onto a sheet pan that has been lightly oiled and dusted with cornmeal. Bake loaf at 500 degrees F (260 degrees C) for 15 minutes. Reduce heat to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Bake for 30 more minutes, or until done. ALL RIGHTS RESERVED © 2019 Allrecipes.com Printed From Allrecipes.com 3/29/2019
