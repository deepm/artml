Recipe By:Carol "This icing is usually put on the wedding cake after the Almond Paste is applied." Ingredients 1-3/4 egg whites 3-1/3 cups confectioners' sugar 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons lemon juice Directions Beat egg whites lightly and add enough sugar to make icing that will hold shape. Blend in lemon juice to get desired consistency. Spread over Almond Paste on cake and let harden. Decorate. ALL RIGHTS RESERVED Â© 2019 Allrecipes.com Printed From Allrecipes.com 3/29/2019
