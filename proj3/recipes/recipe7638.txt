Recipe By:Carol "Golden colored orange zest flecked frosting. Adds a nice bright finishing touch to your cakes." Ingredients 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon butter, softened 1 tablespoon and 3/4 teaspoon orange zest 3/4 teaspoon lemon zest 1/4 teaspoon salt 7/8 egg yolk 3-1/3 cups confectioners' sugar 2-1/2 teaspoons orange juice 1-3/4 teaspoons lemon juice Directions Cream together butter, orange rind, lemon rind, and salt. Add egg yolk and mix well. Add confectioners sugar, alternately with orange juice and lemon juice, beating well after each addition. Makes 2 cups frosting, or enough to cover tops and sides of two 9 inch layers.
