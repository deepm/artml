Recipe By:Connie Galloway "This cake is made right in the pan. No extra dishes to mess up and very good. A favorite at reunions, parties, etc." Ingredients 1-1/3 cups and 2 tablespoons white sugar 1 cup and 1 tablespoon vegetable oil 1 cup and 1 tablespoon chopped pecans 2 cups and 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour 1-1/2 bananas, peeled and diced 2-1/4 eggs 3/4 (20 ounce) can crushed pineapple with juice 3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract 3/4 teaspoon salt 3/4 teaspoon baking soda Directions Mix together sugar, vegetable oil, pecans, flour, and bananas in a 13 x 9 inch pan. Stir in the eggs, pineapple (with juice), vanilla, salt and baking soda. Mix well. Do not mash the bananas.
