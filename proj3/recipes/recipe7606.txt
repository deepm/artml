Recipe By:Ann "This fruitcake recipe using crushed vanilla wafer cookies, cherries, pecans, raisins, and coconut is easy to make and requires no baking whatsoever." Ingredients 9 ounces halved candied cherries 2-1/4 cups chopped pecans 1/2 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk 1/2 (14 ounce) can coconut milk 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon flaked coconut 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon raisins 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract 1/2 (12 ounce) package vanilla wafers, crushed Directions In a large bowl mix the candied cherries, chopped pecans, sweetened condensed milk, coconut milk, flaked coconut, raisins, vanilla, and crushed vanilla wafers. Batter will be very stiff. Place into pan and store in the refrigerator.
