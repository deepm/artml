Recipe By:Peg "My mom use to make this when I was growing up. It's very rich so a little goes a long way. I hope you enjoy it as much as we do." Ingredients 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon white sugar 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon milk 1/4 teaspoon salt 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon pitted dates 1/4 cup and 1 tablespoon chopped walnuts 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract 1/2 teaspoon baking powder 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon packed brown sugar 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon water 2 tablespoons and 1/4 teaspoon butter Directions Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease one 9x13 inch baking pan. Combine the sugar, flour, milk, salt, dates, nut, vanilla and baking powder and mix well. Pour batter into the prepared pan. In a saucepan over medium heat combine the brown sugar, water and butter or margarine and bring to boil. Pour brown sugar mixture over batter. Bake at 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) for 30 to 35 minute or until the cake tests done. Serve warm with whipped cream.
