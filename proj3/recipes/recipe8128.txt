Recipe By:Stephanie "I knew that my husband's favorite cake was spice cake so I made this for him and he absolutely died when he tasted it! It's now his favorite (even better than his mom's recipe!)." Ingredients 1-2/3 cups all-purpose flour 2/3 cup and 2 teaspoons white sugar 3/4 teaspoon baking soda 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg 1/8 teaspoon ground cloves 3/4 teaspoon salt 2/3 cup and 2 teaspoons packed brown sugar 1/3 cup and 2 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon shortening 2/3 cup and 2 teaspoons buttermilk 2-1/4 eggs 1/3 cup and 1 teaspoon shortening 1/3 cup and 1 teaspoon butter, softened 3/4 teaspoon maple flavored extract 2-3/4 cups and 2 tablespoons confectioners' sugar 1 tablespoon and 1-1/4 teaspoons milk Directions Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour two 9-inch cake pans or one oblong pan. Sift flour, white sugar, baking soda, cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves, and salt together. Add brown sugar, 2/3 cup shortening, and buttermilk. Beat 2 minutes. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 30 to 35 minutes or oblong pan for 45 minutes, until toothpick comes out clean. Cool. Frost. To make frosting: cream butter and 1/2 cup shortening together. Add maple flavoring. Gradually add confectioners' sugar. Gradually add milk and continue beating until light and airy.
