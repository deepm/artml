Recipe By:Sandra Bennett "You'll get a gorgeous, dark, and rich chocolate cake from this recipe with prunes, brandy, and ground almonds." Ingredients 7-1/2 (1 ounce) squares bittersweet chocolate 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon unsalted butter 2-1/2 eggs 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons white sugar 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon all-purpose flour 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons finely ground almonds 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon prunes, pitted and chopped 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon brandy 2 tablespoons and 1-1/2 teaspoons water Directions Soak prunes overnight in brandy. Melt chocolate and butter or margarine with water. In a large bowl, beat egg yolks and sugar until pale. Stir in chocolate mixture. Gently mix in flour and ground nuts. Stir in prunes. In another bowl, beat egg whites to stiff peaks. Carefully fold into cake mixture. Pour into a greased 9 inch round cake tin. Bake at 375 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 30 - 40 minutes.
