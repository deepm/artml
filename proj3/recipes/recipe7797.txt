Recipe By:Adora Marchi "A smooth creamy frosting, perfect for any occasion. This is an old recipe from the 1940's." Ingredients 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon all-purpose flour 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon milk 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon shortening 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons white sugar 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon butter, softened 1-3/4 teaspoons vanilla extract Directions In a small saucepan mix the flour and the milk and cook over low heat until it forms a smooth paste with no lumps. Place in refrigerator and let cool completely. With an electric mixer beat the shortening, butter or margarine and the sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in the cooled flour paste and mix until smooth. Stir in the vanilla. Use to frost any cooled cake. Makes enough to frost one 9x13 inch sheet cake or two 8 or 9 inch layer cakes.
