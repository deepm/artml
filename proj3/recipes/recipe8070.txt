Recipe By:Barbara "A fruity filling for a fruity cake. Try it with the Heritage Jam Cake." Ingredients 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon dates, pitted and chopped 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon chopped raisins 7/8 large orange 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon white sugar 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon orange juice Directions Cut orange into sections, and remove the seeds. Grind the sections, peels and all. Combine dates, raisins, and ground orange in a medium saucepan: stir well. Stir in sugar and orange juice. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until mixture is thickened. Spread hot filling between layers or on top of cake.
