Recipe By:LITSTER5 "These muffins are a chocolatey treat thanks to the double chocolate punch of cocoa powder and chocolate chips." Ingredients 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon applesauce 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon margarine, softened 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon white sugar 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon light brown sugar 7/8 egg 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon all-purpose flour 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon unsweetened cocoa powder 1/2 teaspoon baking soda 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon (optional) 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon semi-sweet chocolate chips 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons confectioners' sugar (optional) Directions Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Line muffin cups (2-1/2 inches in diameter) with paper bake cups. In large mixer bowl, beat butter, sugar, brown sugar, egg, and vanilla until well blended. Add applesauce, blend well. In a separate bowl, stir together flour, cocoa, baking soda, and cinnamon, if desired. Add to butter mixture, blending well. Stir in chocolate chips. Fill muffin cups 3/4 full with batter. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 22 to 26 minutes or until wooden pick inserted in center comes out almost clean. Cool slightly in pan on wire rack. Sprinkle muffin tops with confectioners' sugar, if desired. Serve warm.
