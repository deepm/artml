Recipe By:Mariann "A moist nutty cake with the deep flavor of dark rum." Ingredients 1 cup and 3 tablespoons chopped walnuts 7/8 (18.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon dark rum 3-1/4 eggs 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon water 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon vegetable oil 7/8 (3.5 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon butter 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon white sugar 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons water 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon dark rum Directions Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Grease and flour one 10 inch tube or Bundt pan. Sprinkle walnuts over the bottom of the pan. Mix together the yellow cake mix, 1/2 cup dark rum, eggs, 1/2 cup cold water, cooking oil and instant vanilla pudding. Pour batter over nuts. Bake at 325 degrees F (165 degrees C) for 1 hour. Cool, invert on a serving plate and prick the top. To Make Glaze: Melt the butter in a saucepan. Stir in 1/8 cup water and sugar. Boil for 5 minutes, stirring constantly. Remove mixture from heat and stir in rum. Drizzle and smooth glaze evenly over the top and sides of cake. Enjoy!
