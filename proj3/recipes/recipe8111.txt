Recipe By:JJOHN32 "An easy hard sauce to serve over cake." Ingredients 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon butter, softened 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon confectioners' sugar 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon dark rum 3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg Directions Beat butter or margarine until light and fluffy, about 2 minutes. Sift confectioners' sugar into the butter. Add rum, vanilla, and nutmeg. Beat on high speed for 5 minutes. Serve at once over cake, or cover and refrigerate.
