Recipe By:Glenda "No messy clean-up with this one. Just mix and bake in the same pan. Serve with any flavor ice cream." Ingredients 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon all-purpose flour 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons white sugar 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons unsweetened cocoa powder 1-3/4 teaspoons baking powder 1/4 teaspoon salt 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon milk 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons vegetable oil 3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract 1 cup and 3 tablespoons chopped walnuts 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon packed brown sugar 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon unsweetened cocoa powder 1-1/3 cups and 2 tablespoons hot water 1-2/3 cups vanilla ice cream Directions Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). In ungreased 9 x 9 inch square pan stir together flour, sugar, 2 Tablespoons cocoa, baking powder and salt. Mix in milk, oil, and vanilla with fork until smooth. Stir in nuts. Spread evenly in pan. Sprinkle with brown sugar and 1/4 cup cocoa. Pour hot water over batter. Bake 40 minutes. Let stand 15 minutes; cut into squares. Invert each square onto dessert plate. Top with ice cream and spoon sauce over each serving.
