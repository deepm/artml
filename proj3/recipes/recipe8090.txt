Recipe By:Kathy Bliesner "This cake is very rich, and is great to take to gatherings." Ingredients 1/2 (18.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix 1 eggs 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon butter, melted 1/2 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon butter, melted 1 eggs 2-1/4 cups confectioners' sugar Directions Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Grease and flour one 9 x 13 inch pan. In a large bowl, mix together cake mix, 2 eggs, and 1/2 cup melted butter. Put into prepared pan. In another bowl, Beat cream cheese until smooth. Blend in 1/2 cup melted butter and 2 eggs. Gradually beat in the confectioner's sugar. Pour over the crust mixture already in the pan. Bake for 1 hour. Cool.
