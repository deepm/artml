Recipe By:Amy Parsons "This cake was popular during the depression, and does not have eggs in it." Ingredients 1-2/3 cups all-purpose flour 1 cup and 2 tablespoons white sugar 1/2 teaspoon salt 1 teaspoon baking soda 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon unsweetened cocoa powder 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon vegetable oil 1 tablespoon and 1/4 teaspoon distilled white vinegar 1 teaspoon vanilla extract 1 cup and 2 tablespoons cold water Directions Sift flour, sugar, salt, soda, and cocoa together into a 9 x 13 inch ungreased cake pan. Make three wells. Pour oil into one well, vinegar into second, and vanilla into third well. Pour cold water over all, and stir well with fork. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 30 to 40 minutes, or until tooth pick inserted comes out clean. Frost with your favorite icing.
