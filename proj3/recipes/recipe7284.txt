Recipe By:Terry Azose "Yellow cake mix with nutty, chocolate, cinnamon streusel." Ingredients 7/8 (18.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix 7/8 (3.5 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon vegetable oil 3-1/4 eggs 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon plain yogurt 2-1/2 teaspoons unsweetened cocoa powder 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons chopped walnuts 3/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon Directions Beat yellow cake mix, instant vanilla pudding mix, oil, eggs and yogurt together until no lumps remain. In a separate bowl mix cocoa, chopped nuts, and cinnamon together. Spray a large bundt pan with cooking spray and put 1/2 of batter in pan. Sprinkle streusel mix over batter and top streusel with remaining batter. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 50 minutes. Cool for 15 minutes and take out of pan.
