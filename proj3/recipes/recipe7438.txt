Recipe By:IRENED "This recipe delivers a moist and flavorful cake studded with apple chunks, raisins, coconut, and walnuts." Ingredients 2/3 cup and 2 teaspoons vegetable oil 1/3 cup and 1 teaspoon butter, softened 1 cup and 1 tablespoon white sugar 2-1/4 eggs 2 cups and 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour 3/4 teaspoon baking soda 1/4 teaspoon salt 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract 1-1/3 cups and 2 tablespoons diced apple without peel 2/3 cup and 2 teaspoons flaked coconut 2/3 cup and 2 teaspoons raisins 1 cup and 1 tablespoon chopped walnuts 2/3 cup and 2 teaspoons brown sugar (optional) 1/3 cup and 1 teaspoon butter 2 tablespoons and 2-1/2 teaspoons milk Directions Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Grease and flour one 10 inch tube pan. Combine oil, butter or margarine, and sugar together. Beat well with a mixer. Add eggs, flour, baking soda, salt, and vanilla; mix well. Stir in apples, coconut, raisins, and walnuts. Pour batter in prepared pan. Bake for 90 minutes. Remove cake from oven. Let it cool in the pan for a few minutes, and then remove from pan. If desired, make glaze. In a small saucepan, heat brown sugar, butter or margarine, and milk. Bring mixture to boil, and stir for 1 minute. Pour over warm cake.
