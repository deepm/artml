Recipe By:Debbie "Yet another use for your Herman starter. For a variation try making a loaf of bread from your dough. Bake at 325 degrees F (165 degrees C) for 25 to 30 minutes." Ingredients 5/8 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast 1/4 cup and 1 tablespoon warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C) 1 cup Herman Sourdough Starter 1-1/4 cups all-purpose flour 5/8 pinch salt 2 tablespoons and 1-1/2 teaspoons vegetable oil Directions In a large bowl combine yeast and warm water. Stir until yeast is dissolved. Add Herman Sweet Sourdough Starter, flour, salt, and oil. Mix well. Shape into 16 rolls. Place in a greased 8x8 inch pan. Let stand for 30 to 40 minutes. Bake in a 350 degree F (175 degree C) oven for 20 minutes, or until brown.
