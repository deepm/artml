Recipe By:Kelly McRae "Moist chocolate cake that doesn't need frosting." Ingredients 2-1/2 eggs 1-2/3 cups white sugar 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon butter 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon water 1-3/4 cups and 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons unsweetened cocoa powder 3/4 teaspoon baking soda 3/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon 3/4 teaspoon ground allspice 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon semisweet chocolate chips 1-2/3 cups green apples 2-1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract Directions Cream together the eggs, sugar, butter or margarine, and water. Add the flour, cocoa, baking soda, ground cinnamon, and ground allspice and mix well. Add chocolate chips and shredded apples (use a cheese grater) and vanilla. Pour into a greased and floured bundt pan. Bake at 325 degrees F (165 degrees C) for 60 to 70 minutes.
