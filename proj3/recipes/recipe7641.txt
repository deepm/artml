Recipe By:Melodie "The gob cake is a Pennsylvanian relative of whoopie pie, combining a chocolate cake with sweet, creamy icing, as done in this recipe." Ingredients 1-1/4 cups all-purpose flour 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon white sugar 1-1/4 teaspoons baking soda 2 tablespoons and 1-1/2 teaspoons unsweetened cocoa powder 1/2 teaspoon salt 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon vegetable oil 1 tablespoon and 3/4 teaspoon distilled white vinegar 3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon water 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon butter, softened 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon shortening 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon milk 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon white sugar Directions Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Mix the flour, 2 cups of the sugar, baking soda, 6 tablespoons of the cocoa, salt, vegetable oil, vinegar, 2 teaspoons vanilla, and the water together. Stir until just combined. Pour batter into on ungreased 13x9 inch baking pan. Bake at 325 degrees F (165 degrees C) for 30 to 35 minutes. Let cake cool than ice. To Make Icing: Beat together the butter or margarine, vegetable shortening, 1 cup sugar, milk, and 1 teaspoon vanilla. Continue beating for 10 minutes. Spread over cooled cake.
