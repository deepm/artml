Recipe By:Ron Evers "This bread is a favourite with our family, either fresh or toasted." Ingredients 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon water 7/8 egg, beaten 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons dry milk powder 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons honey 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons margarine 1-1/4 teaspoons salt 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon bread flour 1-2/3 cups all-purpose flour 3/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon 3/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg 3/4 teaspoon ground ginger 1/2 teaspoon ground allspice 1/2 teaspoon ground mace 1/2 teaspoon ground cardamom 1 teaspoon active dry yeast 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon raisins 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon candied mixed fruit peel Directions Place ingredients in order suggested by your manufacturer. Select sweet bread setting. Add the raisins and mixed peel when the machine beeps, or 5 minutes before kneading is complete.
