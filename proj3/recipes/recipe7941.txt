Recipe By:Laura Owen "This is also called Chocolate Upside Down Cake, because it can be turned out-- duh --upside down. It's good served with whipped cream or vanilla ice cream." Ingredients 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons white sugar 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons butter 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon milk 3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon all-purpose flour 1/4 teaspoon salt 3/4 teaspoon baking powder 2 tablespoons and 1-1/2 teaspoons unsweetened cocoa powder 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon white sugar 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon packed brown sugar 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon unsweetened cocoa powder 1 cup and 1 tablespoon strong brewed coffee, boiling Directions Cream together 3/4 cup sugar and butter. Add the milk and vanilla and mix. Sift together the flour, salt, baking powder, and 3 tablespoons cocoa, and add to the mixture. Spread into a greased 9 x 9 inch pan. The batter will be thick. Combine 1/2 cup white sugar, 1/2 cup brown sugar, and 1/4 cup cocoa. Sprinkle this over the batter. Finally, pour 1 1/4 cups boiling double strength coffee or water evenly over all.
