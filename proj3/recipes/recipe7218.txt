Recipe By:Jill M. "This is a great treat! Very different from the usual bread-machine breads! It is perfect served fresh and warm or toasted and then broiled with a little mozzarella cheese. Enjoy!" Ingredients 1 cup and 2 tablespoons water 2-1/2 cups bread flour 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons dry milk powder 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons white sugar 1-1/4 teaspoons salt 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons butter 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon pepperoni sausage, chopped 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon shredded mozzarella cheese 2-1/2 teaspoons grated Parmesan cheese 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon canned mushrooms 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon dried minced onion 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder 2 teaspoons active dry yeast Directions Place ingredients in bread machine pan in the order suggested by the manufacturer. Select basic bread setting. Start. To bake bread in oven: select dough or manual cycle. Once cycle is complete, shape dough and place in a greased loaf pan. Allow to rise in a warm spot until doubled in size. Bake in a preheated 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) oven for 35 to 45 minutes or until a thermometer inserted in the center of the loaf reads 200 degrees F (95 degrees C).
