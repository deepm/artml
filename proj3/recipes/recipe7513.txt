Recipe By:Suzanne Stull "Cream Cheese glaze on this cake makes the cake a hit." Ingredients 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon butter 1-2/3 cups white sugar 4-1/4 eggs 7/8 (16 ounce) package graham cracker crumbs 1-3/4 teaspoons baking powder 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon milk 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon flaked coconut 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon chopped pecans 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon butter 7/8 (3 ounce) package cream cheese 3-1/3 cups confectioners' sugar 7/8 (8 ounce) can crushed pineapple, drained Directions Cream 1 cup butter or margarine. Add white sugar gradually. Beat until mixture is light and fluffy. Add eggs, one at a time, beat after each addition. Add cracker crumbs and baking powder, alternately with milk. Blend in coconut and nuts. Bake in preheated 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) oven in 3 - 9 inch pans for 25 to 30 minutes. To Make Cream Cheese Glaze: Cream 1/2 cup butter and cream cheese. Add confectioners' sugar, then pineapple. Mix well. Fill and frost layers.
