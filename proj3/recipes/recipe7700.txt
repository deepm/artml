Recipe By:Carol "A tasty coffee flavored whipped cream perfect for fillings and/or frostings." Ingredients 1-1/4 cups heavy whipping cream 1 tablespoon and 3/4 teaspoon white sugar 1-3/4 teaspoons instant coffee powder 1-3/4 teaspoons boiling water Directions Beat whipping cream and sugar together in mixing bowl until stiff. Dissolve coffee granules in boiling water. Cool. Add to whipped cream and beat in. Makes enough to frost and fill one 8 or 9 inch 2 layer cake. Keep Mocha Cream refrigerated.
