Recipe By:Carol "This is a good cake frosting, and is especially good when the maple syrup can be purchased in March. You can use the artificial for those of you who do not live in the north, or cannot obtain it. This recipe will fill and frost a 9 inch cake." Ingredients 1-3/4 egg whites 1 cup and 1 tablespoon packed brown sugar 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon maple syrup 2 tablespoons and 1-1/2 teaspoons cold water 1/8 teaspoon cream of tartar 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon chopped walnuts Directions Place egg whites, brown sugar, maple syrup, water, and cream of tartar in the top of a double boiler over boiling water. Beat with a mixer until frosting has a good spreading consistency, and will stand in peaks. Remove from heat. Fill and frost cake. Sprinkle the top with chopped nuts.
