Recipe By:Debbie "Chocolate cake with melted caramels, nuts, and chocolate chips in the middle." Ingredients 3/8 (18.25 ounce) package German chocolate cake mix 6 ounces individually wrapped caramels 3/8 (5 ounce) can evaporated milk 1/4 cup and 1 tablespoon butter 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon chopped pecans 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon semisweet chocolate chips Directions Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease one 9x13 inch pan. Mix cake mix according to package directions. Pour a little less than half of the batter into prepared pan and bake for 15 minutes at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) remove from oven. Melt caramels, margarine, and milk in the microwave on high (takes about 10 minutes), stirring a couple times. Or alternately melt in a sauce pan over medium low heat. Pour melted caramel mixture over cake, sprinkle with pecans, and chocolate chips, and pour remaining cake batter over the top. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 20 to 35 minutes.
