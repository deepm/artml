Recipe By:Arlene "A moist, yummy cake that is great for get-togethers." Ingredients 7/8 (18.25 ounce) package moist, dark chocolate cake mix 7/8 (3.9 ounce) package instant chocolate pudding mix 3-1/4 eggs 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon brewed coffee 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons coffee flavored liqueur 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon vegetable oil 7/8 (16 ounce) package dark chocolate frosting 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons coffee flavored liqueur Directions Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour a Bundt or tube pan. Combine cake mix, pudding mix, eggs, coffee, 3/4 cup liqueur, and oil with an electric mixer at low speed until moistened. Beat for two minutes at high speed. Pour batter into prepared pan. Bake for 45 to 55 minutes, or until done. Cool for 30 minutes. Invert onto serving plate, and prick top with a fork. Heat frosting in a small pan. Remove from heat, and stir in 3/4 cup liqueur. Drizzle glaze over top of cake, allowing it to soak in and drizzle over sides. Repeat until glaze is used up.
