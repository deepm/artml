Recipe By:JBS BOX "This cake recipe uses pistachio pudding mix in both batter and frosting for delicious cake take on Watergate salad." Ingredients 1/2 (18.25 ounce) package white cake mix 1-3/4 eggs 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon vegetable oil 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon lemon-lime flavored carbonated beverage 1 (3 ounce) packages instant pistachio pudding mix 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon frozen whipped topping, thawed 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon cold milk Directions Combine cake mix, eggs, oil, soda drink and 1 box pistachio pudding. Pour batter into a greased and floured 13 x 9 inch pan. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 30 to 40 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into cake comes out clean. Cool completely. While cake is baking, mix 1 box pistachio pudding, whipped topping, and milk. Chill. Ice cooled cake with chilled frosting.
