Recipe By:Jan Bittner "There are many, fancy variations on this basic recipe. This recipe works with many types of bread - white, whole wheat, cinnamon-raisin, Italian or French. Serve hot with butter or margarine and maple syrup." Ingredients 20 thick slices bread 6-3/4 eggs 2-1/4 cups milk 3/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon (optional) 3/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg (optional) 1 tablespoon and 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional) salt to taste Directions Beat together egg, milk, salt, desired spices and vanilla. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or skillet over medium-high heat. Dunk each slice of bread in egg mixture, soaking both sides. Place in pan, and cook on both sides until golden. Serve hot.
