Recipe By:ELLENMARIE "My family LOVES bread pudding, and this recipe is one that I have fine tuned to their taste. I have to double this recipe, and bake it in a 9x13 inch pan for my family! It's great for breakfast or dessert and is delicious with milk poured on top! Enjoy!" Ingredients 5 slices day-old bread 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons butter, melted 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon raisins (optional) 3-1/4 eggs, beaten 1-2/3 cups milk 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons white sugar 3/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon 3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract Directions Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Break bread into small pieces into an 8 inch square baking pan. Drizzle melted butter or margarine over bread. If desired, sprinkle with raisins. In a medium mixing bowl, combine eggs, milk, sugar, cinnamon, and vanilla. Beat until well mixed. Pour over bread, and lightly push down with a fork until bread is covered and soaking up the egg mixture. Bake in the preheated oven for 45 minutes, or until the top springs back when lightly tapped.
