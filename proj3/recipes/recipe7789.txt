Recipe By:Star Pooley "Quick and easy! Great for potlucks! Better than Brad Pitt? I'll have to think about that!" Ingredients 1/2 (18.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix 1/2 (20 ounce) can crushed pineapple, with juice 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon white sugar 1 (3.5 ounce) packages instant vanilla pudding mix 1-2/3 cups milk 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon heavy whipping cream 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract 2 tablespoons and 3/4 teaspoon confectioners' sugar 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon flaked coconut, toasted Directions Prepare yellow cake as directed, using a 9 x 13 inch pan. Bake until done, and remove from oven. Poke holes in the top of the cake using a fork. In a saucepan, combine pineapple with juices and sugar. Cook over medium heat until syrup thickens. Pour over top of warm cake, and cool. In another bowl, beat milk with pudding. Spread over the pineapple mixture. Beat whipping cream until soft peaks form, and then beat in confectioners' sugar and vanilla. Spread whipped cream over pudding. Sprinkle toasted coconut over the top of the cake. Refrigerate for several hours or overnight.
