Recipe By:Liz "This is a recipe from my grandmother's recipe collection." Ingredients 4-1/4 egg whites 1/4 teaspoon cream of tartar 1/8 teaspoon salt 1/2 cup and 2 teaspoons white sugar 1/3 cup and 1 teaspoon all-purpose flour 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract 4-1/4 egg yolks 1/2 cup and 2 teaspoons white sugar 1/8 teaspoon salt 1/2 cup and 2 teaspoons all-purpose flour 2 tablespoons and 2-1/2 teaspoons boiling water 1/2 teaspoon baking powder 3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract Directions To make Bottom/White part: Beat whites with salt until frothy. Add cream of tartar and continue beating until stiff, but not dry. Sift 1/2 cup flour and 3/4 cup sugar 4 times (separately). Add sugar gradually, then fold in flour. Add in 1/2 teaspoon vanilla. Put into ungreased angel cake pan. Prepare top. To Make Top/Yellow Part: Beat yolks until light. Add 3/4 cup sugar, which has been sifted 4 times, gradually. Beat for 3 minutes. Add 3/4 cup flour and baking powder, which has been sifted together 4 times. Alternate flour mixture with hot water. Add 1 teaspoon vanilla. Pour this mix over top of first batter. Bake at 325 degrees F (165 degrees C) for 40 minutes.
