Recipe By:Alicia "A nice change from a traditional loaf of white bread." Ingredients 3/4 cup and 3 tablespoons buttermilk 2-1/2 cups bread flour 1-1/4 teaspoons salt 1-1/4 teaspoons white sugar 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons shredded sharp Cheddar cheese 1-1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast Directions Place ingredients in bread machine pan in the order suggested by the manufacturer. Select White Bread setting. Start. To bake bread in oven: select dough or manual cycle. Once cycle is complete, shape dough and place in a greased loaf pan. Allow to rise in a warm spot until doubled in size. Bake in a preheated 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) oven for 35 to 45 minutes.
