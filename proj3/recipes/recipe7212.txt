Recipe By:D. Reichel "Fantastic with any Italian dish! I use fresh herbs from my garden when they are available. When using fresh herbs, double the given amount. The smell will make it impossible to wait. ENJOY!" Ingredients 1 cup and 1 tablespoon warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C) 2-1/2 cups bread flour 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons dry milk powder 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons white sugar 1-1/4 teaspoons salt 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons butter, softened 2 tablespoons and 1-1/2 teaspoons grated Parmesan cheese 1-1/4 teaspoons dried marjoram 1-1/4 teaspoons dried thyme 3/4 teaspoon dried basil 3/4 teaspoon dried oregano 2-1/2 teaspoons active dry yeast Directions Place ingredients in bread machine pan in the order suggested by the manufacturer.
