Recipe By:Ladan M Miller "This cinnamon-accented cake with banana, raisins, and nuts is good for year-round entertaining" Ingredients 2 cups and 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour 1-1/3 cups and 2 tablespoons white sugar 3/4 teaspoon baking soda 3/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon 3/4 teaspoon salt 1-1/3 cups and 2 tablespoons diced bananas 2/3 cup and 2 teaspoons corn oil 1 teaspoon vanilla extract 2-1/4 eggs 2/3 cup and 2 teaspoons raisins 1/3 cup and 1 teaspoon water 1/2 cup and 1 teaspoon chopped walnuts Directions Measure flour, sugar, soda, cinnamon, and salt, and sift together. Dice bananas and add to dry ingredients with oil ,vanilla, eggs, raisins, water, and nuts. Pour into a greased 10-inch tube pan. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 1 1/2 hour or until done. Cool on rack before removing.
