Recipe By:Amy Sumerlin "An incredibly rich chocolate cake that needs no frosting." Ingredients 3/8 (18.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix 3/8 (3.5 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix 1-3/4 eggs 3-1/2 ounces sour cream 3-1/4 (1 ounce) squares German sweet chocolate, grated 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon semisweet chocolate chips Directions Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease and flour one 9x13 inch cake pan. Mix all ingredients and pour into prepared pan. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 55 minutes.
