Recipe By:Pepper "This is a recipe I have had since 1954. I use a cream cheese frosting." Ingredients 1-1/3 cups and 2 tablespoons cake flour 1-1/4 cups white sugar 1/2 teaspoon salt 1/2 teaspoon baking powder 1/2 teaspoon baking soda 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon shortening 2-1/2 (1 ounce) squares unsweetened chocolate, melted 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon buttermilk 3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract 1-3/4 eggs 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon shredded coconut Directions Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour two 9 inch, round cake pans. Sift together cake flour, sugar, salt, baking powder, and soda. Add shortening, chocolate, buttermilk, and vanilla; beat for 2 minutes. Add eggs, and beat for 2 minutes. Stir in coconut. Pour batter into prepared pans. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes. Cool, and frost.
