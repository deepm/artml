Recipe By:Carol "Especially good for trimming a cake. It can also be used for frosting. Use any flavoring to compliment the flavor of your cake." Ingredients 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon butter 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon all-purpose flour 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon water 3-1/3 cups confectioners' sugar 1-1/4 teaspoons vanilla extract Directions Cream butter until very light. Cream in flour. Add water and vanilla. Add sugar until desired consistency is reached. For frosting the cake use a thinner icing.
