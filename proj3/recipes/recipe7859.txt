Recipe By:Ruthie "This is the easiest and fastest cheese cake that anyone can make." Ingredients 13 ounces cream cheese 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon milk 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon white sugar 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons cornstarch 3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract 3-1/4 eggs 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon, or to taste Directions Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease one 8x8 inch square pan. Place the cream cheese, milk, sugar, corn starch and vanilla in a blender and puree. Add the eggs one at a time after the last egg has been added blend mixture for 7 minutes. Pour batter into the prepared pan and sprinkle top with cinnamon. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 1 hour. Let cheese cake cool in oven for one hour. Then place in the refrigerator for at least 3 to 4 hours before serving.
