Recipe By:Sally "These are Pennsylvania Dutch Funny Cakes, baked in a pie crust with a chocolate "funny" layer that sinks to the bottom while baking." Ingredients 7/8 (9 inch) unbaked pie crusts 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon white sugar 2 tablespoons and 3/4 teaspoon unsweetened cocoa powder 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon water 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon white sugar 2 tablespoons and 3/4 teaspoon butter 1/2 eggs 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon milk 1/2 teaspoon baking powder 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract 2/3 cup and 1 teaspoon all-purpose flour Directions Combine 1 cup sugar, cocoa, water, and 1 teaspoon vanilla in a saucepan. Heat over medium-low heat to the boiling point, but do not boil. Set aside. In a large bowl, cream together 1 1/2 cup sugar and butter. Beat in eggs. Stir in milk and 1 teaspoon vanilla. Combine flour and baking powder; mix into the creamed mixture. Divide batter into the pastry shells. Pour 1/3 of the cocoa mixture on top of the cake batter in each pastry shell. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 25 to 35 minutes, or till cake is done.
