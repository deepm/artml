Recipe By:Becky "This vanilla-flavored cake icing recipe uses gelatin to thicken the mixture of heavy whipping cream and confectioners' sugar." Ingredients 1 (.25 ounce) package unflavored gelatin 4 teaspoons cold water 1 cup heavy whipping cream 1/4 cup confectioners' sugar 1/2 teaspoon clear imitation vanilla extract Directions Mix gelatin and cold water in a small saucepan, and let the mixture stand until gelatin thickens. Cook over low heat, stirring constantly until gelatin dissolves. Remove from heat, and let cool; do not let set. Whip cream, sugar, and vanilla until slightly thickened. Continue beating slowly, while gradually adding the gelatin. Whip at high speed until stiff.
