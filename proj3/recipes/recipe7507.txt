Recipe By:MaryKaye "I have had this recipe for over 30 years. Cream may be substituted for the milk for added richness." Ingredients 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons butter 2 tablespoons and 1-1/2 teaspoons milk 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon packed brown sugar 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon confectioners' sugar 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract Directions In a saucepan over medium heat, melt the butter, and mix in 3 tablespoons milk and brown sugar. Boil vigorously for 1 minute. Remove from heat, and beat in 1/2 cup confectioners' sugar. Cool slightly, and beat in the vanilla and remaining 1/2 cup confectioners' sugar.
