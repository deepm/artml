Recipe By:Dmarcks "My husband doesn't like rhubarb, but I have fooled him every time with this cake as you cannot taste the rhubarb in it." Ingredients 3/8 (18.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix 1-2/3 cups rhubarb, chopped 3/8 (3 ounce) package raspberry flavored Jell-O(R) mix 1/4 cup and 1 tablespoon white sugar 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon butter 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon water Directions Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Line the bottom of one ungreased 9x13 inch baking pan with the diced rhubarb. Sprinkle the white sugar over the top. Then sprinkle the raspberry gelatin over the top of the sugar. Next sprinkle the cake mix over the top of the gelatin. Melt the butter or margarine and pour it over the top of the cake mix. Pour the water over the top of the cake. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 45 minutes. If you are using frozen rhubarb reduce the amount of water used to 1/2 cup.
