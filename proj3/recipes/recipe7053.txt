Recipe By:Liz Bartholomew "Make an outrageously delightful oatmeal bread from your bread machine" Ingredients 1 cup and 1 tablespoon water 2-1/2 cups bread flour 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons honey 1 tablespoon and 3/4 teaspoon dry milk powder 1-1/4 teaspoons salt 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon quick cooking oats 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons margarine 2-1/2 teaspoons active dry yeast Directions Place ingredients in the pan of the bread machine in the order recommended by the manufacturer. Select White Bread setting, and Start. ALL RIGHTS RESERVED © 2019 Allrecipes.com Printed From Allrecipes.com 3/29/2019
