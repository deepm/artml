Recipe By:Judy Wilson "Start with a devil's food cake mix to deliver this amaretto-flavored pound cake studded with chocolate chips." Ingredients 2-1/2 eggs 7/8 (18.25 ounce) package devil's food cake mix 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon vegetable oil 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon water 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons almond extract 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon semisweet chocolate chips 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon confectioners' sugar for dusting Directions Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease the bundt pan. Mix eggs, cake mix, oil, water and almond extract with electric beater. Stir in chocolate chips. Pour into prepared pan. Bake approximately 1 hour or until cake tests done. Cool, then dust with confectioners' sugar.
