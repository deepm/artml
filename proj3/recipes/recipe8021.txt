Recipe By:Roxy "This is my 90-year-old grandmother's recipe. Very simple." Ingredients 1-1/3 cups and 2 tablespoons packed brown sugar 2 cups and 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour 2-1/4 teaspoons baking soda 1 tablespoon and 1-1/4 teaspoons ground cinnamon 1 tablespoon and 1-1/4 teaspoons ground cloves 1 tablespoon and 1-1/4 teaspoons ground allspice 1 tablespoon and 1-1/4 teaspoons ground nutmeg 2-3/4 eggs 1 tablespoon and 1-1/4 teaspoons grated lemon zest 1 tablespoon and 1-1/4 teaspoons vanilla extract 1/3 cup and 1 teaspoon brandy 1 cup and 1 tablespoon raisins 1-1/2 cups and 1 tablespoon chopped walnuts 1 cup and 1 tablespoon dried mixed fruit 1 cup and 1 tablespoon butter, melted 1-1/4 cups brandy Directions Preheat oven to 225 degrees F (110 degrees C). Grease and flour a tube pan. In a large bowl, combine sugar, flour, soda, cinnamon, cloves, allspice and nutmeg. Add eggs, lemon rind, vanilla, and 1/2 cup brandy. Mix thoroughly. Stir in fruit, nuts, and melted butter. Pour into prepared pan. Bake for 1 hour in the preheated oven, or until a tester inserted in the center comes out clean. Cool on a wire rack.
