Recipe By:Frank De Lisi "A tasty and healthy toasting bread for cold winter mornings." Ingredients 1 cup bread flour 1 1/4 cups whole wheat flour 1/4 cup rolled oats 1/8 cup wheat germ 2 tablespoons honey 7/8 cup milk 1/2 cup water 1 teaspoon salt 1 tablespoon butter 1/4 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast Directions Place ingredients in the bread machine pan in the order suggested by the manufacturer. Select the multigrain setting, and press start.
