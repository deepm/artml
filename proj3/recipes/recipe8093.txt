Recipe By:Olene Grieshop "Get ready to do the hula! Good with cream cheese or sour cream frosting." Ingredients 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon white sugar 1/2 teaspoon baking soda 1 cup and 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour 1 eggs 1/2 (20 ounce) can crushed pineapple with juice 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon chopped walnuts 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon flaked coconut Directions Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease one 9 x 13 inch pan. In a large bowl, mix sugar, soda, and flour by hand. Mix in eggs, pineapple with juice. Stir in nuts, and coconut. Pour into prepared pan. Bake for 35 to 40 minutes. Cool on wire racks.
