Recipe By:Robin "Chocolate cola icing perfect for the Coco-Cola Cake." Ingredients 6 tablespoons cola-flavored carbonated beverage 3 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder 1/2 cup butter 4 cups confectioners' sugar 1 cup chopped walnuts 1 teaspoon vanilla extract Directions Bring to boil butter, cocoa, and cola drink. Remove from heat and stir in confectioners' sugar, chopped nuts, and vanilla. Top cake while hot.
