Recipe By:IRENED "This has a nice fresh lemon flavor. Makes nice muffins, also." Ingredients 1-1/4 cups all-purpose flour 1/2 teaspoon baking soda 1/4 teaspoon salt 1/4 cup and 1 tablespoon butter, softened 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon white sugar 1-1/4 eggs 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon lemon juice 1/4 cup and 1 tablespoon plain yogurt 1 tablespoon and 3/4 teaspoon lemon zest 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon poppy seeds Directions Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Heavily grease and flour two 9x5 inch loaf pans. Sift together flour, baking soda, and salt. Set aside. In a large bowl, cream butter or margarine, and sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in eggs, one at a time. Mix lemon juice and yogurt together and add alternately with the sifted flour mixture to the butter mixture. Mix until just blended. Stir in lemon rind and poppy seeds. Pour mixture into prepared pans and smooth tops. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 50 minutes or until browned and a knife inserted in the center comes out clean.
