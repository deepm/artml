Recipe By:Carol "This cake is a dry and grainy texture with a hint of orange." Ingredients 1/4 cup and 3 tablespoons butter, softened 1 cup and 1 tablespoon white sugar 2-3/4 eggs 1/3 cup and 2 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon sour cream 3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract 1 tablespoon and 1-1/4 teaspoons grated orange zest 1-1/3 cups and 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour 1/3 cup and 2 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon unsweetened cocoa powder 1/8 teaspoon baking soda 1/8 teaspoon salt 1/3 cup and 1 teaspoon chopped almonds Directions In a bowl, cream the butter with the sugar. Beat in the eggs, then the sour cream, vanilla and orange rind. In another bowl, stir together the flour, cocoa, baking soda and salt. Beat into the creamed mixture just until incorporated and then stir in the almonds. Turn the batter into a greased and floured tube pan. Bake in a 350 degrees F (175 degree C) oven for 1 hour, or until it tests done with a toothpick. Let cool on a rack. Makes 16 servings.
