Recipe By:Jennifer Gurule "This is as quick and easy as cheesecake recipes get, using cookies and graham crackers for a crust in a no-bake cheesecake." Ingredients 3/4 cup and 3 tablespoons finely ground graham cracker crumbs 3/4 cup and 3 tablespoons pecan sandies cookies 3 tablespoons and 2-1/4 teaspoons butter 3 tablespoons and 2-1/4 teaspoons white sugar 10 ounces cream cheese 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon white sugar 2 tablespoons and 1-1/2 teaspoons lemon juice 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons heavy whipping cream, whipped 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons sliced fresh strawberries (optional) Directions In a bowl, mix together crushed cookies and graham crackers with melted butter and the 3 tablespoons sugar. Press into a 7 inch springform pan. Place in refrigerator until ready for use. In another bowl, beat cream cheese, 1/3 cup of sugar and lemon juice. Whip cream, and fold into cream cheese mixture. Spread into pan. Top with sliced strawberries (optional). Freeze for 1 hour, covered with foil. Place in refrigerator 30 minutes before serving.
