Recipe By:Kaye Frickhoeffer "Lemon Bundt(R) cake gets a tropical twist thanks to the addition of Key lime juice and lemon pudding mix to the batter." Ingredients 3/8 (18.25 ounce) package lemon cake mix 3/8 (3 ounce) package instant lemon pudding mix 1/4 cup and 1 tablespoon water 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon vegetable oil 1-3/4 eggs 2 tablespoons and 1/4 teaspoon key lime juice 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons confectioners' sugar 1 tablespoon and 3/4 teaspoon key lime juice Directions Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease one 9x13 inch cake pan or one 10 inch bundt pan. In a large bowl stir together; lemon cake mix, lemon instant pudding, water, oil, eggs, and 5 tablespoons key lime juice, mixing well. Pour batter into prepared pan. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 45 minutes. Pour key lime glaze over cake while still warm. To Make Glaze: Mix together the confectioner's sugar and 3 tablespoons of the key lime juice (or more if necessary), spoon glaze over warm cake.
