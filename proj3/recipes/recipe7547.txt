Recipe By:Muffy Nasner "This is the best cheese cake ever, a true New York cheese cake!" Ingredients 1-1/4 cups graham cracker crumbs 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon white sugar 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon butter, melted 4-1/4 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened 4-1/4 eggs 1-3/4 egg yolks 1-1/3 cups and 2 tablespoons white sugar 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons all-purpose flour 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon heavy whipping cream Directions Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Mix the graham cracker crumbs, 1/2 cup of the white sugar, and the melted butter together. Press mixture into the bottom of one 9 or 10 inch springform pan. In a large bowl, combine cream cheese, eggs and egg yolks; mix until smooth. Add the remaining 1 3/4 cups white sugar, the flour and the heavy cream. Blend until smooth. Pour batter into prepared pan. Bake at 400 degrees F (200 degrees C) for 10 minutes, then turn oven temperature down to 200 degrees F (100 degrees C) and continue baking for 1 hour, or until filling is set.
