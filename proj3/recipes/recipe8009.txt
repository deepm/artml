Recipe By:Shawna Wallis "I leave the coconut and nuts out, and it is still very good. I get rave reviews when I take it anywhere!" Ingredients 1/2 (18.25 ounce) package white cake mix 1/2 (3 ounce) package instant pistachio pudding mix 1-3/4 eggs 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon lemon-lime flavored carbonated beverage 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon vegetable oil 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon flaked coconut (optional) 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon chopped walnuts (optional) 1/2 (3 ounce) package instant pistachio pudding mix 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon milk 1/2 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed Directions Mix together cake mix, 1 box pudding mix, eggs, oil, and soda drink. Stir in coconut and nuts if desired. Pour into a greased 9 x 13 inch baking pan. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 35 to 40 minutes. Cool. Beat 1 box pudding and milk together.
