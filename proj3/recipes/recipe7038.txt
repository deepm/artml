Recipe By:Laura "Delicious, easy-to-make biscuits." Ingredients 2 cups and 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons white sugar 1-1/4 teaspoons baking powder 1/2 teaspoon baking soda 1/2 teaspoon salt 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon shortening 3/8 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C) 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon buttermilk 2-1/2 teaspoons butter, melted Directions Dissolve yeast in warm water. Sift together flour, sugar, soda, baking powder, and salt. Add buttermilk and dissolved yeast. Add shortening and stir. Store dough, covered, in the fridge until ready to use. Roll out on a lightly floured surface or shape into balls--these do not need to rise. Brush tops of biscuits with melted butter. Bake at 400 degrees F (205 degrees C) for 15-20 minutes.
