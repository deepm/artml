Recipe By:Syd "This is a simple recipe but it makes a rich chocolate cake thanks to the addition of grated chocolate and sour cream." Ingredients 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon milk 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon grated semisweet chocolate 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons white sugar 7/8 eggs 1/4 cup and 1 tablespoon sour cream 1/2 teaspoon baking soda 1-1/4 teaspoons water 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon cake flour 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon butter, softened Directions Preheat oven to 350 degree F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour one 9 x 13 inch pan. Grate the chocolate. In a small saucepan, boil the milk and chocolate until mixture forms a custard. Cream the butter or margarine and sugar. Add eggs and sour cream to the creamed mixture. Dissolve the baking soda in water, and add to the creamed mixture. Add the flour, and mix thoroughly. Add the chocolate mixture and beat well. Pour into a greased and floured 9x13 inch pan. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 40 minutes to one hour.
