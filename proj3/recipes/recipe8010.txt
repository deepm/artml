Recipe By:JENNY AMAR "This is a wonderful eggless cake recipe, moist and absolutely delicious. Not only that, it hardly takes any time to prepare. It's something I got from my aunt, and whip up in a jiffy. I hope you love it too!" Ingredients 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon butter 7/8 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk 2 cups self-rising flour 3/4 cup and 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder 1-3/4 teaspoons baking powder 3/4 teaspoon baking soda 7/8 (12 fluid ounce) can or bottle cola-flavored carbonated beverage Directions Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Butter and dust with flour a 10 inch bundt tin. Cream the butter or margarine till light and fluffy. Beat in the condensed milk. Combine the flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, and baking soda; mix into the creamed mixture alternately with the cola. Bake for 45 minutes, or until the cake tests done.
