Recipe By:CANDACEMARIE "I make this cake over and over. It is easy to put together and the frosting of the cake is easy and fast. It goes fast around my house." Ingredients 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon white sugar 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon all-purpose flour 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon butter 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon vegetable oil 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon water 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons unsweetened cocoa powder 1/2 teaspoon baking soda 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon buttermilk 7/8 eggs 1/2 teaspoon salt 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon butter 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons unsweetened cocoa powder 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons milk 1-2/3 cups confectioners' sugar 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon chopped pecans Directions Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease and flour one 9 x 13 inch pan. Sift 2 cups white sugar and 2 cups flour together; set aside. In a sauce pan; combine 1/2 cup butter or margarine, oil, water, and 1/4 cup cocoa. Bring mixture to a boil, remove from heat and add to dry ingredients. Dissolve baking soda in buttermilk; add with eggs, salt, cinnamon and 1 teaspoon vanilla to cocoa, flour mixture and stir well. Pour batter into a 9 x 13 inch pan. Bake at 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) for 20 minutes. To Make Frosting: Five minutes before cake is done combine 1/2 cup butter,1/4 cup cocoa and 1/4 cup milk in saucepan and bring to a boil. Remove from heat and stir in confectioners' sugar, 1 teaspoon vanilla, and chopped pecans. Pour frosting over hot cake. Cake remains moist and may be frozen. Serves 20.
