Recipe By:Mari "A whole wheat sourdough bread for the bread machine. The whole wheat flour adds a nice flavor and texture to your basic sourdough bread." Ingredients 1-1/4 cups sourdough starter 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C) 1 tablespoon and 3/4 teaspoon margarine 1 tablespoon and 3/4 teaspoon white sugar 1/2 teaspoon salt 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons whole wheat flour 2-1/2 cups all-purpose flour 1-1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast Directions Add ingredients in order suggested by your manufacturer. Select whole wheat setting.
