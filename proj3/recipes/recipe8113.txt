Recipe By:Rachael "A delicious, easy-to-make maple frosting, great on white cakes." Ingredients 7/8 (8 ounce) package cream cheese 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon butter 3-1/3 cups confectioners' sugar 3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract 1/2 teaspoon maple flavored extract Directions Cream together the cheese and butter or margarine. Stir in the confectioner's sugar and flavorings. Mix well.
