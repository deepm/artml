Recipe By:Jennifer Houde "This recipe is for the bread machine. It is similar to homemade grandmother's sweet bread." Ingredients 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon milk 7/8 egg 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons margarine 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon white sugar 1/2 teaspoon salt 2-1/2 cups bread flour 2 teaspoons active dry yeast Directions Add ingredients in order suggested by your manufacturer. Select "sweet bread" setting.
