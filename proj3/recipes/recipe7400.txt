Recipe By:Stephanie "This cake is made with three layers: Cake, filling, and topping. There are 4 types of milk in the filling and topping (whole milk, condensed milk, evaporated milk, and heavy cream). This is an excellent cake for milk lovers!" Ingredients 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour 1/2 teaspoon baking powder 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon unsalted butter 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon white sugar 2 eggs 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon whole milk 3/8 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk 3/8 (12 fluid ounce) can evaporated milk 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons heavy whipping cream 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon white sugar 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract Directions Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour one 9x13 inch baking pan. Sift flour and baking powder together and set aside. Cream butter or margarine and the 1 cup sugar together until fluffy. Add eggs and the 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract; beat well. Add the flour mixture to the butter mixture 2 tablespoons at a time; mix until well blended. Pour batter into prepared pan. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 30 minutes. Pierce cake several times with a fork. Combine the whole milk, condensed milk, and evaporated milk together. Pour over the top of the cooled cake. Whip whipping cream, the remaining 1 cup of the sugar, and the remaining 1 teaspoon vanilla together until thick. Spread over the top of cake. Be sure and keep cake refrigerated, enjoy!
