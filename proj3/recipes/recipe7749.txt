Recipe By:Leo J. Lagrotte "Use this creamy vanilla frosting recipe when you need a quick solution for your cakes or cupcakes." Ingredients 1/2 cup butter, softened 1/2 cup shortening 2/3 cup confectioners' sugar 1/2 cup milk 5 tablespoons all-purpose flour 1 teaspoon vanilla extract Directions Combine butter or margarine, shortening, confectioners' sugar, flour, vanilla, and milk in a mixing bowl. Whip all ingredients until fluffy.
