Recipe By:Barbara "This bread is wonderful and sweet. Omit the raisins for just a plain good cinnamon bread." Ingredients 1 cup and 1 tablespoon warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C) 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons margarine, softened 2-2/3 cups and 1 tablespoon bread flour 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon white sugar 1-1/4 teaspoons salt 3/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon 1 tablespoon active dry yeast 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons raisins Directions Place ingredients in order suggested by your manufacturer. Select the sweet bread and light crust settings. Add raisins when indicated by your manufacturer.
