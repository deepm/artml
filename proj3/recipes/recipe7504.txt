Recipe By:v monte "This cake recipe is very good and quite simple due to the use of a cake mix, but it's also also very fattening!" Ingredients 7/8 (18.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon white sugar 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons vegetable oil 3-1/4 eggs 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon chopped pecans 3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon sour cream 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon packed brown sugar 2-1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon Directions Mix together the cake mix, sugar, oil, eggs, pecans, and vanilla. Fold in the sour cream. Put half the batter in a bundt pan. Mix the brown sugar and cinnamon and sprinkle over the batter in the pan. Pour the remainder of the batter on top.
