Recipe By:Sandy "This sweet Portuguese bread is made in the bread machine." Ingredients 2/3 cup milk 1-1/4 eggs 1-1/4 teaspoons margarine 3 tablespoons and 1-3/4 teaspoons white sugar 1/2 teaspoon salt 2 cups and 3 tablespoons bread flour 1-3/4 teaspoons active dry yeast 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon lemon juice Directions Place ingredients in bread machine pan in the order suggested by the manufacturer. Select white bread setting. Start. To bake bread in oven: select Dough cycle. Once cycle is complete, shape dough and place in a greased loaf pan. Allow to rise in a warm spot until doubled in size.
