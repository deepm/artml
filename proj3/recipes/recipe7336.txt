Recipe By:Karen Birchfield "A lovely fruit cocktail cake, simple and delicious." Ingredients 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon all-purpose flour 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon white sugar 1/2 teaspoon salt 3/4 teaspoon baking powder 7/8 egg 7/8 (16 ounce) can fruit cocktail 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon packed brown sugar Directions Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Spray one 8 x 8 inch square baking pan with cooking spray. In a medium sized bowl combine flour, white sugar, salt, baking powder, egg, and fruit cocktail. Mix until just combined and pour into the prepared pan. Sprinkle top with the brown sugar. Bake at 32 degrees F (165 degrees C) for 45 minutes or until golden brown.
