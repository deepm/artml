Recipe By:knowell "Very good!!! The extra sugar makes for a better rise." Ingredients 3/4 cup and 3 tablespoons water 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons molasses 2-1/2 teaspoons vegetable oil 3/4 teaspoon salt 1-2/3 cups all-purpose flour 1-1/4 cups rye flour 2 tablespoons and 1-1/2 teaspoons packed brown sugar 2-1/2 teaspoons unsweetened cocoa powder 1/2 teaspoon caraway seed 1-3/4 teaspoons bread machine yeast Directions Place ingredients in the pan of the bread machine in the order recommended by the manufacturer. Select the Basic cycle, and press Start.
