Recipe By:JJOHN32 "This recipe for a no-bake cake made with cookies, chocolate, raisins, almonds, and cherries is great for a children's party." Ingredients 1/2 cup butter 2 tablespoons molasses 2 tablespoons white sugar 4 (1 ounce) squares semisweet chocolate 1 1/2 cups roughly broken vanilla wafers 1/4 cup raisins 1/4 cup sliced almonds 1/2 cup candied cherries Directions Put the butter or margarine, molasses, sugar, and chocolate into a 1-1/2 quart oven-proof glass bowl and microwave on high for two minutes or until fat and chocolate have melted. Stir well. Add the broken cookies or wafers, raisins, almonds, and cherries. Mix well to coat them with the chocolate mixture. Place a 7 inch plain flan ring on a flat serving plate. Spoon in the cookie mixture. Press down evenly. Chill in refrigerator for about 8 hours or overnight. Remove flan ring and serve.
