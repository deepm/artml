Recipe By:Rick Mazzuca "Use this recipe to get an ideal buttercream for frosting and borders on cakes, cupcakes, or even cookies." Ingredients 1-2/3 cups shortening 6-2/3 cups confectioners' sugar 1/2 teaspoon salt 1-3/4 teaspoons vanilla extract 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon heavy whipping cream Directions In a mixing bowl, cream shortening until fluffy. Add sugar, and continue creaming until well blended. Add salt, vanilla, and 6 ounces whipping cream. Blend on low speed until moistened. Add additional 2 ounces whipping cream if necessary. Beat at high speed until frosting is fluffy.
