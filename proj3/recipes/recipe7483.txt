Recipe By:Deb "Very moist and delicious. Makes a great brunch dessert." Ingredients 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon butter, softened 1-1/4 cups packed brown sugar 7/8 egg 2-1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract 1-3/4 cups and 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour 3/4 teaspoon baking soda 1/2 teaspoon salt 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon sour cream 3-1/3 cups chopped rhubarb 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon white sugar 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg Directions In large bowl, cream together butter or margarine and brown sugar. Beat in egg and vanilla. Sift or stir together flour, baking soda and salt; gradually stir in to butter mixture. Fold in sour cream and rhubarb. Spoon batter into greased 9 x 13 inch glass baking dish. Stir together white sugar and nutmeg; sprinkle over batter. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 40 minutes, or until tester comes out clean.
