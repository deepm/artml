Recipe By:jamie "This excellent recipe uses cake mix and whiskey to make a very moist and tasty yellow cake." Ingredients 7/8 (18.25 ounce) package moist white cake mix 1/2 cup and 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons chopped walnuts 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon butter 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon white sugar 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon whiskey Directions Mix cake batter as directed on box. Stir in nuts. Bake cake as directed on box in a bundt pan. Before taking cake out of oven, combine butter, sugar, and whiskey. Boil for 5 minutes. Remove syrup from heat, and pour over cake. Continue baking cake for 5 more minutes. Cool in pan for about 15 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack to cool.
