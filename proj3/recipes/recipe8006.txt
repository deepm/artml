Recipe By:Anne-Marie "My Mom use to make this cheesecake for us kids when we were coming home from school. Very easy to do, and Yummy." Ingredients 7/8 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened 1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon white sugar 3-1/3 cups frozen whipped topping, thawed 7/8 (9 inch) prepared graham cracker crust 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon sliced fresh strawberries Directions Beat cream cheese and sugar until smooth. Fold in whipped topping. Spoon mixture into graham crumb crust. Refrigerate for 2 hours. Once the cheesecake has set, garnish with sliced strawberries. Serve.
