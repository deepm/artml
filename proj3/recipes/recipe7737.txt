Recipe By:Carol "This recipe features an icing made with cream cheese, cocoa, and cinnamon frosts this dark chocolate cake for an elegant dessert." Ingredients 1-2/3 cups boiling water 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon unsweetened cocoa powder 2-1/4 cups and 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour 1-3/4 teaspoons baking soda 1/2 teaspoon baking powder 1/2 teaspoon salt 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon butter, softened 1-3/4 cups and 2 tablespoons white sugar 3-1/4 eggs 1-1/4 teaspoons vanilla extract Directions Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease 3 - 9 inch round cake pans. In medium bowl, pour boiling water over cocoa, and whisk until smooth. Let mixture cool. Sift together flour, baking soda, baking powder and salt; set aside. In a large bowl, cream butter and sugar together until light and fluffy. Beat in eggs one at time, then stir in vanilla. Add the flour mixture alternately with the cocoa mixture.
