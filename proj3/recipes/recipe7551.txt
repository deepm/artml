Recipe By:Suzanne Stull "Very moist cake that is very good for a pot luck dinner." Ingredients 3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon all-purpose flour 1/2 teaspoon baking soda 7/8 eggs 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons white sugar 1/4 teaspoon salt 3/8 (20 ounce) can crushed pineapple with juice 1/4 cup and 1 tablespoon butter 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon white sugar 3 tablespoons and 1/2 teaspoon evaporated milk 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract Directions Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease one 9x13 inch pan. Mix the flour, baking soda, salt, and 1 1/2 cups of the white sugar together. Stir in the beaten eggs and the pineapple with juice and mix well. Pour batter into prepared pan. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 30 minutes. Pour Buttery Milk Syrup over top of cake while still warm. To Make Buttery Milk Syrup: In a sauce pan combine the butter or margarine, evaporated milk, vanilla, and 1/2 cup white sugar.
