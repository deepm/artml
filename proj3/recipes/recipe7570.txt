Recipe By:Carol "This is a strange name for a cake, but it is very good." Ingredients 2 cups and 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour 1-1/3 cups and 2 tablespoons white sugar 3/4 teaspoon baking soda 1/4 teaspoon salt 3/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon 3/4 cup and 2 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon vegetable oil 2-1/4 eggs 3/4 (8 ounce) can crushed pineapple with juice 1-1/3 cups and 2 tablespoons diced bananas 2/3 cup and 2 teaspoons chopped pecans 3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract Directions Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour a 12 cup Bundt pan. Measure flour, sugar, soda, salt, cinnamon, oil, eggs, vanilla into mixing bowl. Beat until smooth. Stir in pineapple with juice, bananas, and pecans. Pour into prepared pan. Bake in oven for about 70 minutes. Turn cake out onto rack or plate after cooling for 20 minutes. Cool, and ice with cream cheese icing.
