Recipe By:Karen "This filling complements chocolate cakes especially well. This recipe makes enough to fill one 2 layer cake." Ingredients 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon packed brown sugar 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon cornstarch 1/4 teaspoon salt 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon water 2-1/2 teaspoons butter Directions Mix together the brown sugar, corn starch, salt, and water in a small saucepan. Bring mixture to a boil, and stir constantly for 1 minute. Remove from heat, and add the butter or margarine. Allow the mixture to cool slightly, and spread between layers.
